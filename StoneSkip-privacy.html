Stone Skip

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Stone Skip is a local-only stone-skipping physics game for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Short answer: we collect nothing.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Stone Skip does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, account)
  • No location data
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No contacts or calendar data
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No network requests of any kind

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All game data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Best skip count and total throw history (last 30 throws as JSON)
  • Stone collection unlock progress
  • Language preference (English or Simplified Chinese)
  • Haptic feedback on/off setting

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Stone Skip uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or backend services. There are zero external dependencies.

5. Data Security

Because no data is transmitted externally, there is no network-level security risk. Local data is protected by iOS standard sandboxing and device encryption.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all local game data at any time via Settings → Reset Records within the app, or by uninstalling Stone Skip from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Stone Skip does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will update this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
