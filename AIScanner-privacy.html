AIScanner Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Overview

AIScanner ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection

AIScanner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing occurs entirely on your device.

Camera Access

The App requires camera access to provide real-time object detection, text recognition, and face analysis features. Camera data is:

  • Processed entirely on-device using Apple CoreML and Vision frameworks
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Discarded immediately after real-time processing

Photo Library Access

The App requests permission to save to your Photo Library only when you choose to capture screenshots or record video. Saved content remains entirely on your device under your control.

Microphone Access

The App requests microphone access only during screen recording sessions. Audio data is saved locally to your device and is never transmitted externally.

On-Device AI Processing

All artificial intelligence and machine learning processing is performed locally on your device using Apple's CoreML framework. No images, video frames, or analysis results are ever sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

The App stores only your user preferences (such as display settings) locally on your device. No personal information is stored.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
