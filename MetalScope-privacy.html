MetalScope - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Overview

MetalScope is a GPU benchmarking application that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

MetalScope does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No data leaves your device

Local Data Storage

MetalScope stores benchmark results and device information locally on your device using SwiftData. This data includes:

  • Benchmark scores (compute, render, bandwidth)
  • Device model and GPU information
  • Test timestamps and iOS version at time of test

This data never leaves your device and is solely used to display your benchmark history and comparisons within the app.

Third-Party Services

MetalScope does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

MetalScope does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
