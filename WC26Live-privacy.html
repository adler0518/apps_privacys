WC26 Live — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

Summary: WC26 Live collects no personal data, does not require login, has no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

WC26 Live is a fully offline app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required.
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, device ID) are collected.
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking is performed.
  • No third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking pixels are integrated.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your local device and is never transmitted:

  • Favorite teams
  • Virtual coin balance and bet history (no real money involved)
  • Match predictions you submit
  • Personal display preferences (timezone, dark mode)

Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

3. Network Access

WC26 Live may make limited network requests to fetch publicly available match data from sports data providers (such as TheSportsDB or football-data.org). These requests contain only standard HTTP headers and do not include any personal information.

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app sends local notifications (goal alerts, kickoff reminders) for your favorite teams. All scheduling happens on your device. We do not use push servers.

5. Children's Privacy

WC26 Live is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app inherently complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar regulations.

6. No In-App Purchases or Real-Money Betting

The "Fantasy Bet" feature uses entirely virtual coins for entertainment. No real money is involved, no in-app purchases are offered, and no payment information is collected or processed.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

8. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete on our side. You retain full control over local data by managing the app on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features change. Updated versions will be posted at this URL with a revised "Effective Date".
