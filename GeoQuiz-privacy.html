Privacy Policy

GeoQuiz · Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GeoQuiz ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy. GeoQuiz is a 100% offline geography quiz application that does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

GeoQuiz is designed with privacy as a first principle. We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Account credentials (no registration is required)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising IDs)
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Contacts, calendars, or reminders
  • Health, fitness, or financial data
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Crash reports or analytics that identify you

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device using local iOS storage. It never leaves your device:

  • Quiz history — Your past quiz scores and results
  • Preferences — Settings such as default difficulty, timer enabled/disabled, language preference
  • Streak counter — Your current quiz streak count

This data is private to you. You can clear it at any time using the "Reset All Scores" feature in Settings, or by deleting the App.

4. Third-Party Services

GeoQuiz does not integrate with any third-party services. The App does not contain:

  • Analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs (no AdMob, no AppLovin, etc.)
  • Crash reporting SDKs (no Crashlytics, no Sentry, etc.)
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud services (no iCloud sync, no remote servers)

The App does not make any network requests during normal operation.

5. Data Security

Since GeoQuiz does not collect or transmit any data, there is no transmission security risk. All locally stored data is protected by iOS's built-in app sandboxing and file-system protection mechanisms.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with the App:

  • Open the App and tap "Reset All Scores" in Settings to clear quiz history; or
  • Delete the App from your device. iOS will automatically remove all locally stored data.

No further action is required. We do not retain any copy of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

GeoQuiz is suitable for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Updates will be posted at the same URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
