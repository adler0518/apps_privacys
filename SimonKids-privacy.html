Privacy Policy

Simon Kids  ·  Last updated: May 20, 2026

No Data Collected

1. Introduction

Simon Kids ("we", "the app") is a children's memory and sound game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect — and the short answer is: none.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Simon Kids does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any information from children under 13

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores only the following data locally on your device:

  • Your best streak score for each game mode
  • Game settings preferences (sound, language)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Simon Kids uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services. The app operates entirely offline with no network connections.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Simon Kids is designed for children. We strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children of any age. The app requires no account creation, no login, and no internet connection.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to secure externally. All local data (game scores and settings) is stored securely in iOS's sandboxed app storage.

7. Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall Simon Kids from your device. All locally stored scores and settings will be permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
