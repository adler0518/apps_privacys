Privacy Policy

App: KidPomo Pro  |  Developer: Xiao Du  |  Effective date: May 13, 2026

Overview

KidPomo Pro is a 100% offline children's daily routine tracker. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All task data, progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account registration or login
  • No names, email addresses, or any identifiable information
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to third-party servers
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No photos or camera access

Local Storage Only

Task lists, star counts, badge progress, and streaks are saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data. We have no access to this data.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

KidPomo Pro is designed for use by children with parental supervision. We do not collect any personal information from children or adults. The app is fully COPPA compliant.

Third-Party Services

KidPomo Pro does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social features.

Network Access

KidPomo Pro does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and publish the revised policy at the same URL.
