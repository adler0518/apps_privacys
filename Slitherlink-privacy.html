Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Summary: Slitherlink is a 100% offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device.

1. Introduction

Slitherlink ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. Since our app is completely offline and does not collect any data, your privacy is inherently protected.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what information we do not collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
  • No Location Data: We do not track your location or access location services
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or collect analytics data
  • No Photos or Media: We do not access your photos, camera, or media library
  • No Financial Information: We do not collect payment information or financial data
  • No Contacts: We do not access your contacts or social networks
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or technical specifications

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game Progress: Your current puzzle state and progress
  • Best Times: Your completion times for different difficulty levels
  • Game Statistics: Number of completed puzzles per difficulty

All this data is stored using iOS local storage mechanisms (UserDefaults) and never leaves your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Starting a new game (which resets current progress)

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

The app is built entirely with native iOS frameworks (SwiftUI, Foundation, Combine) and contains no external dependencies that could collect or transmit data.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

All game data stored on your device is protected by iOS security mechanisms and Apple's app sandboxing.

6. Data Deletion

All data associated with the App is stored locally on your device. To delete all data:

  • Simply delete the App from your device
  • This will permanently remove all game progress, statistics, and settings
  • No data is stored on any server, so there is nothing to delete from our end

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information or data from users of any age, children's privacy is fully protected.

We do not:

  • Collect personal information from children
  • Display targeted advertising
  • Share any data with third parties

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
