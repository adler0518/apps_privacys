IronPulse does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Introduction

IronPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness and workout tracking application developed by xiadu. This Privacy Policy describes how IronPulse handles your information when you use our application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely offline without collecting, transmitting, or sharing any of your personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

IronPulse does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following types of data:

  • Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Health & Fitness Data: We do not use HealthKit or collect any health data from Apple Health.
  • Location Data: We do not track or collect your location.
  • Contact Information: We do not access your contacts.
  • Photos & Media: We do not access your photo library.
  • Financial Information: We do not collect any payment or financial data.
  • Usage Data: We do not use any analytics SDKs to track your app usage.
  • Device Identifiers: We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or tracking data.
  • Audio Data: We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data. Microphone access is used solely for real-time voice interaction during workouts.

IronPulse does not make any network requests. The app functions entirely offline.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated by IronPulse is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults API. This includes:

  • Workout records and training history
  • User preferences and app settings
  • Achievements and progress tracking
  • Configuration data

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party system. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

iCloud Sync: IronPulse does not use iCloud or any cloud synchronization service. Your data remains exclusively on the device where it was created.

4. Permissions Used

IronPulse requests the following system permission:

Permission Purpose Data Collected
Microphone Used for voice interaction features during workout sessions No data stored or transmitted

Important: The microphone is used only for real-time voice interaction during training. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere. You can deny this permission at any time through your device Settings; the app will continue to function with reduced voice interaction capabilities.

5. Third-Party Services

IronPulse does not integrate with any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media SDKs
  • Cloud storage services

The app contains no network calls and operates entirely offline.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted over any network, your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • iOS sandboxing and data protection
  • Device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID
  • File system encryption provided by iOS

Because the app does not transmit any data over networks, there is no risk of data interception during transmission.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can delete all app data by uninstalling IronPulse from your device. This will permanently remove all locally stored data.
  • Manage Permissions: You can manage app permissions at any time through Settings > Privacy on your iOS device.
  • No Account Required: IronPulse does not require user registration or account creation.

8. Children's Privacy

IronPulse does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's use of fitness applications.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
