Introduction

Photo Puzzle Master ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our photo puzzle game application.

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your Photo Library solely for the purpose of selecting images to use as puzzle source material. Photos you select are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external service. The PhotosUI framework is used to present the system photo picker, which provides a secure, sandboxed way to select images.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Leaderboard entries — completion time, move count, difficulty level, and photo source identifier for each puzzle completed
  • Game preferences — selected difficulty level

All of this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external server. You can delete all stored data at any time by deleting the app or clearing app data in iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

Photo Puzzle Master does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. The app makes no external network calls.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in UserDefaults system, which benefits from iOS's native security protections including device encryption and app sandboxing.

Data Deletion

All data associated with Photo Puzzle Master is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. No data is retained on any server after deletion.

Children's Privacy

Photo Puzzle Master does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed as a casual puzzle game suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
