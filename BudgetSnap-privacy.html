Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Budget Snap ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Budget Snap stores all financial data (transactions, budgets, categories, recurring bills, savings goals) locally on your device using an on-device database. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers.

Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your Photo Library solely to save monthly recap images that you explicitly choose to export. No photos are uploaded to any server.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account managed by Apple. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Budget Snap does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data resides on your device. Deleting the App removes all associated data. You can also delete individual records within the App at any time.

Children's Privacy

Budget Snap does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
