Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Summary: ChordBank does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data is stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no network access.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChordBank! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our guitar chord learning application.

Our core principle: ChordBank is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ChordBank does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Contact lists or social media data
  • Health or fitness data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

ChordBank stores the following data locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Practice Progress: Your chord learning status (Not Started, Learning, Practiced, Mastered)
  • Practice Sessions: Records of your practice sessions with duration timestamps
  • Favorites: Chords you've marked as favorites
  • App Settings: Your preferences (audio enabled, left-handed mode, finger display, strum speed)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you delete the app.

4. Third-Party Services

ChordBank does NOT use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services. The app operates entirely offline with no network connectivity required.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults, your data benefits from iOS's built-in security features:

  • Data is stored in the app's sandboxed container
  • Protected by iOS app sandboxing
  • Encrypted when device encryption is enabled
  • Accessible only by the ChordBank app

6. Data Deletion

All data is automatically deleted when you delete the app. Since we store no data on external servers, simply removing ChordBank from your device permanently removes all associated data.

If you wish to clear your practice data without deleting the app, you can use the "Clear All Practice Data" option in the app's Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

ChordBank does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since our app does not collect any personal data whatsoever, this policy applies equally to users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
