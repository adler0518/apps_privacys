Cube Tutor Solver Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-02

Cube Tutor Solver is an offline-first cube learning app. It does not require an account and does not upload cube photos.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information, location, contacts, financial information, analytics identifiers, or usage data.

Camera Permission

The camera permission is used only to capture the six faces of a cube on your device. Images are processed locally for color confirmation and manual correction.

Data Stored on Device

Cube state, selected size, color choices and restore demo progress may be stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes this data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party tracking SDKs or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for learning use and does not knowingly collect data from children.
