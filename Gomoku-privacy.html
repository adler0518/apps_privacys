Privacy Policy

Gomoku · Effective Date: May 27, 2026

Overview

Gomoku ("the App") is a fully offline board game. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we do — and do not — handle.

Data We Collect

None. Gomoku does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The App has no user accounts, no sign-up, and no analytics.

Network Access

Gomoku makes no network requests. The App functions completely offline.

Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it:

  • Game settings (AI difficulty, forbidden rule, haptics, sound, hints)
  • Local statistics (wins, losses, draws)

You may erase all local data at any time by tapping "Reset Statistics" in the Stats tab, or by uninstalling the App.

Tracking and Advertising

The App contains no tracking technologies, no third-party SDKs that collect user data, and no advertising.

Children's Privacy

Gomoku is appropriate for all ages and does not collect any information from any user, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
