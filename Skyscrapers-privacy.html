Privacy Policy

Skyscrapers Logic Puzzle
Last Updated: May 15, 2026
Summary: Skyscrapers is a 100% offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Skyscrapers ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information in connection with your use of our iOS application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear: Skyscrapers does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers
  • Financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or any personal content

The App contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking mechanisms.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated during your use of the App is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Game Progress: Your current puzzle state (cell values, notes, difficulty level)
  • Game Records: Best completion times and number of completed games per difficulty level

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. If you delete the App from your device, all locally stored data is automatically removed.

4. Third-Party Services

Skyscrapers does not integrate with any third-party services.

The App:

  • Does not use any analytics services (e.g., Firebase, Flurry)
  • Does not contain advertising SDKs
  • Does not use social media integrations
  • Does not communicate with any external servers
  • Has zero network dependencies

The App is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks (SwiftUI) and contains no third-party code.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your information is protected by:

  • iOS's built-in app sandboxing
  • Device-level security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Apple's App Store review and security guidelines

We have no servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that could be breached.

6. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • Deleting the App from your device automatically removes all associated data
  • No data persists after app deletion
  • No action is required on your part to request data deletion

7. Children's Privacy

Skyscrapers is suitable for users of all ages. The App:

  • Does not collect any personal information from children or adults
  • Does not contain advertising or in-app purchases
  • Does not include social features or communication capabilities
  • Is designed to be safe for children to use without parental concerns about data collection

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
