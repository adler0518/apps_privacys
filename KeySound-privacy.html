Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

KeySound does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All recordings and analysis data remain on your device.

Information We Collect

KeySound does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Microphone Access

KeySound requests microphone access solely to record mechanical keyboard sounds for audio analysis. Recordings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

Data Storage

All data created within KeySound is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Sound recordings (WAV audio files)
  • Spectrum analysis data (FFT results)
  • Archive metadata (keyboard name, switch type, etc.)
  • User preferences (recording settings)

This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

KeySound offers a one-time Pro unlock purchase processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all transaction data.

Analytics and Tracking

KeySound does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage or behavior.

Third-Party Services

KeySound does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs beyond Apple's native frameworks (StoreKit for in-app purchases).

Children's Privacy

KeySound does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
