TongueGym Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

TongueGym ("we", "our", or "the app") is a coffee palate training application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use TongueGym.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

TongueGym is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal identification information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No network requests of any kind

3. Data Stored on Your Device

TongueGym stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Training progress and quiz scores
  • Taste radar profile data
  • Streak and daily practice records
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

TongueGym does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline.

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's built-in data protection (SwiftData). The data is protected by your device's passcode and encryption.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Data" option in Settings within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device (removes all associated data)

7. Children's Privacy

TongueGym does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
