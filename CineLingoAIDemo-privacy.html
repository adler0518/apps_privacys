Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Movie Language Learning ("CineLingo", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

CineLingo is designed with privacy first. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers.

  • Videos and Media: Videos you import are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to any server.
  • Study Data: Your study statistics, learning history, vocabulary notes, and progress data are stored locally in a SQLite database on your device.
  • Photo Library: We request access to your photo library solely to allow you to import video files for language learning. We do not access, collect, or transmit any photos or videos from your library to external servers.

Data Storage

All data generated by CineLingo — including imported videos, subtitle files, study records, and vocabulary notes — is stored exclusively on your device. No data is uploaded to or stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

CineLingo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

CineLingo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
