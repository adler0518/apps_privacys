Plane War — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

1. Summary

Plane War is a fully offline, single-player iPhone game. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. Period.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account credentials
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) used for tracking
  • No analytics, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs

3. Local Data Storage

Game progress (high score, farthest stage, enemies defeated, bosses defeated, settings) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time via Settings → Reset Progress.

4. Network Access

Plane War does not include any network entitlements. The app does not connect to any server, CDN, or remote service.

5. Children's Privacy

Plane War is suitable for general audiences. Since we collect no data, the protection requirements under COPPA and similar regulations are inherently satisfied.

6. Permissions in Info.plist

Plane War declares zero NSUsageDescription keys. The app cannot and does not request access to any sensitive iOS resource.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top of this page will be revised. As long as the app remains fully offline, the substance will not change.

8. Contact

For questions, email: 544627835@qq.com

霓虹飞机大战 — 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026-05-18

1. 摘要

霓虹飞机大战是一款完全离线的单人 iPhone 游戏。我们不收集、不存储、不共享、不传输任何个人数据。仅此而已。

2. 我们不会收集的数据

  • 姓名、邮箱、电话、账号凭证
  • 位置、通讯录、相册、麦克风、相机访问权限
  • 用于追踪的设备标识符（IDFA、IDFV）
  • 统计、崩溃上报、第三方 SDK

3. 本地数据存储

游戏进度（最高分、最远关卡、击毁敌机、击杀 Boss、设置项）通过 UserDefaults 存储在设备本地，永不离开设备。您可随时通过 设置 → 重置进度 清除。

4. 网络访问

霓虹飞机大战不包含任何网络权限声明。应用不连接任何服务器、CDN 或远程服务。

5. 儿童隐私

本应用面向所有年龄段。由于我们不收集任何数据，COPPA 及类似法规所要求的保护义务自然满足。

6. Info.plist 权限声明

霓虹飞机大战未声明任何 NSUsageDescription 键，因此无法且不会请求访问任何 iOS 敏感资源。

7. 政策变更

本政策若有变更，将更新顶部日期。只要应用保持完全离线，实质内容不会改变。

8. 联系方式

有疑问请邮件联系：544627835@qq.com
