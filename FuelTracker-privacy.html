Privacy Policy

Fuel Tracker  ·  Effective Date: May 13, 2026

Summary: Fuel Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays exclusively on your device.

1. Introduction

Fuel Tracker ("the App") is a fuel consumption and vehicle cost tracking application for iPhone. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, contacts, or any media

There are no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or advertising frameworks integrated into the App.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores fuel fill-up records locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults:

  • Odometer reading (km)
  • Fuel volume (liters)
  • Unit price (per liter)
  • Date and time of each fill-up
  • Vehicle nickname (if set)

This data is stored exclusively on your iPhone and is never transmitted to any server.

4. Network Usage

Fuel Tracker makes zero network requests. The App functions entirely offline and does not communicate with any external server, API, or third-party service.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks. There is no advertising, no tracking, and no data sharing of any kind.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms, including device encryption and passcode protection. We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest iOS version.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete individual records within the App at any time. Uninstalling Fuel Tracker permanently removes all associated data from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Fuel Tracker does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13. The App is fully compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version here with a new effective date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
