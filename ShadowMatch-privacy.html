Shadow Match

Privacy Policy  |  Effective Date: 2026-05-21

1. Introduction

Shadow Match ("the App", "we", "us") is a drag-and-drop shadow puzzle game developed by Xiadu. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

100% Offline   Shadow Match operates entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection at any time.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Shadow Match does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Location data (GPS or approximate)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, serial number)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Camera, microphone, or photo library access
  • Contacts, calendar, or health data
  • Financial or payment information

The App has zero network connections — it never contacts any server, API, or third-party service.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores only the following data locally on your device:

  • Game Progress — Which puzzle levels you have completed. Stored in the app's sandbox using UserDefaults.

This data never leaves your device. It is used solely to allow the App to remember your progress between sessions.

4. Third-Party Services

Shadow Match does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social login systems. There are no third-party services of any kind.

5. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed through network transmission. Game progress is stored in the standard iOS app sandbox, which is protected by the iOS security model.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by Shadow Match, simply delete the App from your device. All locally stored game progress will be permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

Shadow Match is a family-friendly puzzle game suitable for all ages. The App does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under the age of 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App's functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
