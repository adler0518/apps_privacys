Privacy Policy — QuadVault

Last updated: June 3, 2026

QuadVault ("the App") is a digital garage for FPV drone pilots to manage parts inventory, builds, batteries, and spending. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information. In short: the App collects no personal data and works entirely offline.

1. Information We Collect

QuadVault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

2. Local Data Storage

Your parts, builds, batteries, repair logs, and spending records are stored only on your device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the App removes all of this data.

3. Notifications

QuadVault may request permission to send local notifications (for example, battery retirement reminders). These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

4. Permissions

Aside from optional local notifications, QuadVault does not request access to your camera, microphone, photos, location, or contacts. It needs no other special permissions to function.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, cloud servers, or external APIs. It functions completely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of exposure. All app data remains on your device under the protection of iOS.

8. Your Rights

You retain full control of your data. You can delete all locally stored information at any time by removing the App from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
