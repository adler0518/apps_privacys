EditionForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

EditionForge is a limited edition print management app for artists. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No network requests

3. Data Stored on Your Device

EditionForge stores edition series, print records, buyer information, and sales data locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

EditionForge does not integrate any third-party services or SDKs. The app functions entirely offline.

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData with device-level encryption protection.

6. Data Deletion

Delete the app to remove all data, or use in-app data management features.

7. Children's Privacy

EditionForge does not collect data from any user, including children.

8. Changes to This Policy

Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

10. Apple App Privacy Labels

Data Not Collected. EditionForge does not collect any user data.
