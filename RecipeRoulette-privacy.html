Recipe Roulette

Privacy Policy — Last updated: 2026-05-20
No Data Collected

1. Introduction

Recipe Roulette ("the App") is a fully offline recipe discovery app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect — and the answer is: none.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or any network calls whatsoever.

  • No account or sign-in required
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No internet connection needed

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All app data (favorites, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults or local storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Recipe Roulette does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or external APIs. All recipe content is bundled within the app.

5. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted off-device, there is no data in transit to secure. All local data is protected by iOS's standard sandboxing and device encryption.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall Recipe Roulette from your device. All locally stored preferences and favorites will be permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

Recipe Roulette does not collect any personal information from users of any age, making it safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
