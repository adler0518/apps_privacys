ClipVideo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

ClipVideo ("we," "our," or "the App") is a video-intensive listening learning tool designed to help language learners improve their listening comprehension by importing and studying local videos sentence by sentence.

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipVideo handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and have designed the App to function entirely on your device without collecting any personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ClipVideo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, altitude, or any geolocation information
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, feature usage statistics, or session analytics
  • Diagnostic Data: Crash logs, performance metrics, or device telemetry
  • Contacts: Any data from your address book or contact list
  • Health & Fitness: Any health-related or biometric information
  • Financial Information: Payment details, credit card numbers, or transaction history
  • Search History: Any records of your in-app searches or browsing behavior

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated through your use of ClipVideo is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database within the App's designated storage directory. This includes:

  • Video Library: Records of imported videos including file names, paths, and durations
  • Learning Progress: Your study sessions, playback history, and time spent on each video
  • App Settings: Your preferences for playback speed, display options, and other configurations

None of this data is transmitted off your device, synced to cloud services, or accessible to us or any third party.

4. Photo Library Access

ClipVideo requests access to your photo library (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription) solely for the purpose of allowing you to import video files into the App for listening practice. This access is:

  • Initiated only when you choose to import a video
  • Limited to reading video files you explicitly select
  • Never used to scan, upload, or otherwise process your photos automatically

You may deny this permission at any time through your device's Settings app. Denying access will prevent you from importing new videos but will not affect your existing study data.

5. Third-Party Services

ClipVideo does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or external APIs. The App is built exclusively with Apple's native frameworks (SwiftUI, AVFoundation, SQLite3) and operates entirely offline.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms, including:

  • iOS sandboxing: Each app's data is isolated from other apps
  • Device encryption: If your device uses a passcode, all data is automatically encrypted
  • No network transmission: Data never leaves your device, eliminating network-based risks

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all ClipVideo data by:

  • Within the App: Use the in-app deletion features to remove individual videos or learning records
  • System-level: Deleting the ClipVideo app from your device will permanently remove all associated local data

Since no data is stored on our servers, no additional account deletion process is required.

8. Children's Privacy

ClipVideo does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App contains no advertisements, in-app purchases, social features, or external links. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App's functionality or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of ClipVideo after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
