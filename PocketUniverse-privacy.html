Privacy Policy

Pocket Universe · Effective Date: May 20, 2026

Summary: Pocket Universe does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All content is generated and stored locally on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Pocket Universe ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Pocket Universe is designed as a fully offline application. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other personal files
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • App preferences: Your selected language and display settings, stored via UserDefaults
  • Simulation state: Current scenario selection, stored temporarily in memory

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Pocket Universe does NOT integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. There are:

  • No analytics frameworks (no Firebase, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media SDKs
  • No server-side components

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach from our end. All app data resides exclusively on your device, protected by iOS system-level security (encryption at rest, app sandboxing).

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with Pocket Universe, simply uninstall the App from your device. Since no data is stored on external servers, uninstallation removes all traces of the App completely.

7. Children's Privacy

Pocket Universe is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information whatsoever, we are inherently compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations worldwide.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version at this URL and update the effective date. Since the App does not collect data, significant changes are unlikely.
