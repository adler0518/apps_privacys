MathDash - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Overview

MathDash is a mental math training game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Data Collection

MathDash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress, scores, achievements, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SwiftData).

No Account Required

MathDash does not require user registration, login, or any form of account creation. You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information.

No Network Communication

MathDash operates entirely offline. The app does not make any network requests, does not connect to external servers, and does not transmit any data from your device.

No Third-Party Services

MathDash does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

No Special Permissions

MathDash does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive device features.

Children's Privacy

MathDash is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's data.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data by using the "Reset All Progress" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
