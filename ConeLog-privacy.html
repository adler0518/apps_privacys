Privacy Policy — ConeLog

Last updated: June 3, 2026

ConeLog ("the App") is a personal training logger for freestyle slalom (cone) inline skating — letting you record practice sessions, track which tricks you are attempting or have mastered, browse a trick library, and review your practice streaks and statistics. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information. In short: the App collects no personal data and works entirely offline.

1. Information We Collect

ConeLog does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

2. Local Data Storage

Your practice sessions, trick mastery records, notes, and statistics are stored only on your device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the App removes all of this data.

3. Permissions

ConeLog does not request access to your camera, microphone, photos, location, or contacts. It needs no special permissions to function.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, cloud servers, or external APIs. It functions completely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of exposure. All app data remains on your device under the protection of iOS.

7. Your Rights

You retain full control of your data. You can delete all locally stored information at any time by removing the App from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
