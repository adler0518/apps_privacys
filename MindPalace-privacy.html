Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026 · Effective Date: May 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Mind Palace ("we", "our", or "the App") is a memory training tool built around the classical Method of Loci. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles and how it is handled.

Mind Palace is a fully offline application. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data with us or with any third party.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be very clear about this. Mind Palace does NOT:

  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account information.
  • Collect or transmit your location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or any device sensor data.
  • Use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks.
  • Send any of your inputs (memory pegs, palace contents, tournament records) to a remote server.
  • Use cookies, web beacons, or fingerprinting.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App needs to store the following information locally on your device in order to function. This data never leaves your device:

  • Memory palaces you create, including their themes, names, and the memory pegs (text content, hints, generated imagery cues, ordering).
  • Tournament records from your practice sessions (palace name, accuracy, duration, score, timestamp).
  • App preferences such as the language used by the system and basic UI state.

This data is stored using Apple's standard local storage mechanism (UserDefaults) inside the App's sandboxed container.

4. Third-Party Services

None. Mind Palace does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporters, or cloud services. The App makes no network requests as part of its normal operation.

5. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally inside the App's sandbox on your device, it is protected by the standard iOS sandboxing and on-device encryption provided by Apple. No data is transmitted, so there is no transmission attack surface.

6. Data Deletion

You can remove all data created by Mind Palace at any time:

  • Use in-app controls to delete individual palaces or tournament records.
  • Or simply uninstall the App. iOS will remove the App's container, including every byte of data Mind Palace has stored.

7. Children's Privacy

Mind Palace is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because we do not collect any personal data, the App complies with COPPA and similar regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated through the App's release notes.
