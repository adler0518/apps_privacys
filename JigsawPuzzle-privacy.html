Privacy Policy

JigsawPuzzle - Last Updated: May 2026

Summary: JigsawPuzzle does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data and user-imported images are stored 100% locally on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to JigsawPuzzle ("we", "our", or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our sliding puzzle game application.

Our core principle: Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

JigsawPuzzle is designed with privacy-first architecture. We explicitly DO NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Contacts or social data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage mechanisms:

3.1 Game Records

  • Puzzle completion time
  • Number of moves
  • Difficulty level selected
  • Selected puzzle image preference

Storage method: UserDefaults (JSON serialization)

3.2 User-Imported Images

  • Photos you choose to import from your device's photo library
  • Images are processed locally (scaled, cropped, compressed)
  • Stored in your app's Documents directory

Important: These images never leave your device. We do not upload them to any server.

3.3 Data Deletion

All locally stored data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app from your device.

4. Permissions Used

JigsawPuzzle uses the following system permissions:

Permission Purpose Required?
Photos (via PhotosUI) Allow you to select photos from your library for custom puzzles Optional

Note: We use iOS PhotosUI framework, which provides a system-level photo picker. The app only receives the photo you explicitly select. We do not request full photo library access.

5. Third-Party Services

JigsawPuzzle does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services.

The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks (SwiftUI, UIKit, Foundation) and contains no external dependencies.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is protected by iOS sandbox security model
  • No network transmission means no risk of interception
  • No server infrastructure means no risk of data breaches

7. Children's Privacy

JigsawPuzzle is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, this policy inherently protects children's privacy.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
