Privacy Policy

BreatheDown - Breathing Exercise & Stress Relief

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BreatheDown ("we", "our", or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

BreatheDown is a breathing exercise and stress relief app that helps you relax through scientifically-backed breathing patterns. With real-time heart rate tracking via Apple Watch, you can see the calming effects of your practice.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we do not collect:

  • No personal information - We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifiable information
  • No account registration - The app does not require you to create an account or log in
  • No analytics or tracking - We do not use any analytics SDKs or tracking technologies
  • No advertising data - We do not serve ads or collect advertising identifiers
  • No cloud storage - Your data is never transmitted to any server
  • No financial data - We do not collect payment or financial information through the app
  • No location data - We do not access or store your location
  • No contacts data - We do not access your contacts
  • No photos/videos - We do not access your photo library

No Data Collection No Analytics No Server Communication

3. Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

BreatheDown uses Apple's HealthKit framework to access your heart rate data. This is how we handle your health information:

3.1 What We Read

  • Heart Rate - We read your real-time heart rate from Apple Watch during breathing sessions to show you how your heart rate changes as you practice

3.2 What We Write

  • Practice Records - We can write your breathing practice records to the Health app, so you can track your stress relief progress alongside your other health data

3.3 Your Control

  • You grant HealthKit permissions through Apple's system authorization screen
  • You can revoke these permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy → Health
  • We only access the specific data types listed above, nothing more

4. Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated by BreatheDown is stored locally on your device:

4.1 Practice Records

Your breathing practice history is stored locally, including:

  • Date and time of each session
  • Breathing mode used (4-7-8, Box, or Resonance)
  • Practice duration
  • Heart rate data points during the session
  • Calculated metrics: peak heart rate, minimum heart rate, average heart rate, baseline heart rate

4.2 App Preferences

Your settings are stored locally using UserDefaults:

  • Default breathing mode
  • Preferred practice duration
  • Haptic feedback intensity
  • Language and theme preferences

5. Apple iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your BreatheDown data may be included in your iCloud backup as part of your device's local storage. This is managed by Apple's iCloud service and is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

You can manage iCloud settings for your device in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage.

6. Third-Party Services

BreatheDown does not integrate with any third-party services. The app uses only Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • HealthKit - For reading heart rate data and writing practice records
  • SwiftUI - For user interface
  • AVFoundation - For voice guidance
  • CoreHaptics - For haptic feedback

No third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external services are used.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in sandbox protection
  • Health data is accessed through Apple's secure HealthKit framework
  • The app does not transmit any data over the network
  • iOS automatically encrypts data on devices with a passcode enabled
  • No data is shared with or accessible to any third party

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual records - Remove any practice record from within the app
  • Delete all data - Uninstalling the app will remove all data stored by BreatheDown from your device
  • Revoke Health permissions - You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings
  • Delete Health data - Practice records written to the Health app can be deleted through the Health app

Since no data is stored on any server, deletion is immediate and complete once performed on your device.

9. Children's Privacy

BreatheDown is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect any personal information and requires no account registration, it is safe for use by children under parental supervision.

Breathing exercises are generally safe for children, but we recommend consulting with a healthcare provider before children use breathing exercise apps.
