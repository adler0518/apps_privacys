BreatheDown

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

BreatheDown ("we," "our," or "the App") is a breathing exercise application that uses Apple Watch heart rate data to provide real-time biofeedback during breathing sessions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using BreatheDown, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

BreatheDown does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on remote servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Photos or Videos: The app does not access your photo library or camera.
  • Location Data: The app does not access or track your location.
  • Financial Information: The app does not process payments or store financial data.
  • Usage Analytics: The app does not track your usage patterns or send analytics to third parties.
  • Identifiers: The app does not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any form of personal identifier.
  • Contact Information: The app does not access your contacts, email, or phone number.
  • Health Data (transmitted): While the app reads heart rate data from Apple HealthKit, this data is never transmitted off your device.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

BreatheDown stores the following data exclusively on your device using local file storage (JSON format in the app's Documents directory):

  • Practice Records: Date, duration, breathing mode selected, heart rate data points collected during each session, calculated stress drop percentage, and baseline heart rate.
  • User Preferences: Selected breathing mode, practice duration, haptic feedback intensity, language preference, and theme preference (stored via UserDefaults).

All data is deleted automatically when you delete the app from your device. No data persists beyond the lifetime of the app installation.

4. Permissions Used by This App

BreatheDown requests the following system permissions. You may deny any permission; the app will continue to function with limited features.

Permission Purpose Required?
HealthKit — Read Heart Rate Read real-time heart rate data from Apple Watch during breathing exercises to display heart rate trends. No — the app functions without it, but heart rate features will be unavailable.
HealthKit — Write Practice Data Write breathing practice records to the Apple Health app for your personal tracking. No — you can deny this and still use all in-app features.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings app under Health > Data Access > Apps > BreatheDown.

5. Third-Party Services

BreatheDown does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or data-sharing platforms.

The app uses only Apple-provided frameworks:

  • HealthKit: For reading heart rate data and writing practice records to Apple Health. Data is read from and written to your local HealthKit store only.
  • WatchConnectivity: For detecting Apple Watch connection status (no data is transmitted between devices).
  • CoreHaptics: For haptic feedback during breathing sessions.
  • AVFoundation: For voice guidance audio playback.

None of these frameworks transmit data to our servers or any third-party servers.

6. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, your data benefits from the built-in security protections of iOS, including:

  • Device Encryption: iOS encrypts all user data when a passcode is set.
  • App Sandbox: Each app's data is isolated and inaccessible to other apps.
  • No Network Transmission: Since the app sends no data over the network, there is no risk of interception during transmission.

7. Data Deletion

Deleting the app deletes all data. When you remove BreatheDown from your device:

  • All practice records stored in the app's local JSON file are permanently deleted.
  • All user preferences stored in UserDefaults are permanently deleted.
  • Practice records previously written to Apple Health remain in Apple Health (controlled by Apple, not by us). You can manage these through the Apple Health app.

There is no separate "delete my data" function because no data exists outside your device.

8. Children's Privacy

BreatheDown is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the app does not collect or transmit any data, it does not pose a privacy risk to children. Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of HealthKit-enabled features.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of BreatheDown after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
