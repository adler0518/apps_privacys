VoxLoop Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

VoxLoop ("we", "our", or "the app") is a multi-track beatbox loop station for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data VoxLoop handles and how it is stored.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

VoxLoop does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No financial or payment information
  • No browsing or search history
  • No contacts or address book data
  • No health or fitness data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

VoxLoop stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Audio Recordings: Recorded beatbox tracks are saved as CAF audio files in the app's local Documents directory.
  • Project Metadata: Project names, track settings (volume, pan, EQ), and creation dates are stored in a local SwiftData database on your device.

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

4. Microphone Access

VoxLoop requires microphone access to record audio. This permission is requested at runtime and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings. Audio captured by the microphone is processed locally and saved only to your device's storage.

5. Third-Party Services

VoxLoop does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools. The app makes zero network requests.

6. Data Security

All data is protected by iOS's built-in app sandboxing. Each app's data is isolated from other apps and the operating system. VoxLoop relies on Apple's hardware and software security measures to protect your recordings.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete individual projects within the app at any time. Uninstalling VoxLoop from your device permanently removes all associated data, including audio recordings and project metadata. There is no cloud backup or remote storage.

8. Children's Privacy

VoxLoop does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user regardless of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
