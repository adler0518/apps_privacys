Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Summary: AquaQuest is a 100% offline app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your health information stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to AquaQuest ("we", "our", or "the app"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our hydration tracking application.

Our fundamental principle: Your data belongs to you. AquaQuest operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

AquaQuest does not collect any of the following:

  • ✗ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ✗ Location data
  • ✗ Contact lists
  • ✗ Photos or media
  • ✗ Usage analytics or crash reports
  • ✗ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ✗ Financial information
  • ✗ Health data shared with third parties
  • ✗ Browsing or search history

3. Data Stored on Your Device

AquaQuest stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • 💧 Water intake records (amount, date, time)
  • 📊 Daily goals and achievement status
  • 🌱 Plant growth stage and health metrics
  • 🏆 Achievement unlock status
  • ⚙️ App settings (reminder preferences, goal amounts)

This data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your iOS device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Accessible only to the AquaQuest app
  • Automatically deleted when you delete the app

4. Permissions Used

AquaQuest requests the following system permissions:

  • Notifications: Used solely for sending hydration reminders at times you configure. You can grant or deny this permission without affecting core app functionality. Reminder data is processed locally on your device.

All permissions can be managed through your device's Settings app. The app functions fully even if you deny optional permissions.

5. Third-Party Services

AquaQuest does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

The app is built exclusively with Apple's native frameworks:

  • SwiftUI (user interface)
  • SwiftData (local data storage)
  • UserNotifications (local reminders)

No third-party code has access to your data or device.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is managed by iOS:

  • Data is protected by iOS app sandboxing
  • Device encryption (when passcode is enabled) protects stored data
  • No network requests means no data interception risk
  • No cloud storage means no server breach risk

7. Data Deletion

Deleting the app automatically deletes all your data.

Since no data is stored on external servers, simply removing AquaQuest from your device permanently erases:

  • All water intake records
  • Achievement progress
  • Plant growth status
  • App settings and preferences

No additional steps are required. No data persists after app deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

AquaQuest is suitable for users of all ages. The app:

  • Does not collect personal information from children
  • Does not display targeted advertising
  • Does not include social features or communication
  • Functions safely in offline mode

Parents can confidently allow children to use this app without privacy concerns.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since the app operates offline, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

Material changes will be communicated through:

  • App Store release notes
  • In-app notification (when app is opened)
