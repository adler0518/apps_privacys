Privacy Policy

Qix — Last updated: May 2026

Zero Data Collection

1. Introduction

Qix ("we", "our", "the App") is a territory-claiming arcade game for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains that we collect no personal data whatsoever.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Network activity or browsing data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard UserDefaults API:

  • Level unlock progress (which levels you have completed)
  • Per-level best territory percentage
  • Cumulative total score
  • App settings (sound, haptics preferences)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we cannot access it.

4. Network Access

The App requires no network connection and makes no network requests. All gameplay and data storage is 100% offline.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or social sign-in services.

6. Data Security

Because no data is transmitted outside your device, there is no risk of interception or server-side breach. All locally stored data is protected by iOS device encryption.

7. Data Deletion

All locally stored game data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App. You can also reset progress at any time through the in-app settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. It complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App's features change. Any updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: adler0518@gmail.com

11. Apple App Privacy Labels

In accordance with Apple App Store requirements, our privacy label is: Data Not Collected. We do not collect any data linked or linkable to your identity.
