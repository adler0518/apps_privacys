Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Introduction

WordWeave ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of our iOS application.

Important: WordWeave is a 100% offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

WordWeave does NOT collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Financial information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts or social data
  • Photos or media (except as explicitly imported by you for app functionality)

Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated by WordWeave is stored locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Your scores, completed games, and statistics are saved locally using iOS UserDefaults
  • Settings: Your app preferences and configuration choices are stored locally
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not use iCloud or any cloud storage services
  • No User Accounts: No registration or login is required

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

WordWeave does NOT integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or SDKs that collect user data.

The app functions entirely independently without any external network communication.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms:

  • App sandboxing prevents other apps from accessing WordWeave's data
  • iOS encryption protects data at rest
  • No network transmission means no risk of data interception

Data Deletion

All WordWeave data is automatically and completely deleted when you:

  • Uninstall the app from your device
  • Reset your device to factory settings

Since we don't collect or store any data on our servers, there is no additional deletion process required. Simply removing the app ensures all your data is permanently deleted.

Children's Privacy

WordWeave is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 or any other age group.

The app is designed to be educational and appropriate for children learning English vocabulary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
