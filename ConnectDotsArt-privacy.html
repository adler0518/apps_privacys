Connect Dots Art

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: 2026-05-20

1. Introduction

Connect Dots Art ("we", "our", or "the App") is a dot-to-dot puzzle game developed for entertainment and educational purposes. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects (or does not collect), how we handle information, and your rights as a user.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Connect Dots Art does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect Data Not Collected

Connect Dots Art does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or camera access
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts or social information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Advertising identifiers

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's UserDefaults (AppStorage):

  • Puzzle completion status (which puzzles you have finished)
  • Your selected language preference (English or Chinese)
  • Voice guidance on/off setting
  • Difficulty level preference (Easy, Medium, Hard)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Connect Dots Art uses no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or social login services. The App does not communicate with any external servers.

The only Apple system framework used for audio is AVSpeechSynthesizer (text-to-speech), which processes text entirely on-device and does not transmit any data.

5. Internet Access

Connect Dots Art requires no internet connection. The App functions entirely offline. We do not make any network requests.

6. Children's Privacy

Connect Dots Art is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal data, the App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Parents and guardians can allow children to use this App with confidence that no personal information is collected or transmitted.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches involving Connect Dots Art. All preferences stored locally on your device are protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms.

8. Data Deletion

All locally stored preferences are automatically deleted when you uninstall Connect Dots Art from your device. You can also reset preferences at any time through the App's Settings screen.
