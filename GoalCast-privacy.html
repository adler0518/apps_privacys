GoalCast - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

Overview

GoalCast ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Match predictions you submit within the App
  • Your scoring history and performance statistics
  • Favorite teams you select
  • App preferences (language, theme, notification settings)

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Photo Library Access:

The App may request access to your photo library solely to save prediction share cards that you generate. We do not access, read, or upload any existing photos from your library.

Third-Party Services

Crash Reporting (Sentry):

The App uses Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports. This data includes technical information about the crash (stack traces, device model, OS version) and does not include any personal information or user-generated content.

Sports Data:

Match schedules, scores, and team information are sourced from public sports data APIs. This data is publicly available factual information and does not involve any personal data collection.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. All user data remains on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Deleting the App from your device
  • Clearing App data in your device settings

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: goalcast.support@outlook.com

GoalCast is not affiliated with or endorsed by any football governing body.
