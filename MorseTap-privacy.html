Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Summary: MorseTap is a 100% offline Morse code learning app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information remains on your device.

1. Introduction

MorseTap is an iOS application designed to help users learn and practice Morse code. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains completely private.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

MorseTap operates entirely offline and does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device information
  • Financial information
  • Contacts or social media data
  • Photos or media files

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Practice sessions: Your Morse code practice attempts, including input text, decoded output, accuracy scores, and timestamps
  • User preferences: Audio playback speed settings and mode selections
  • Learning progress: Statistics on completed practice sessions

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

MorseTap does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app contains no SDKs that collect or transmit data.

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your iOS device using SwiftData, Apple's secure local database framework. The data is protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms and is only accessible to the MorseTap app on your device.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by MorseTap:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, or
  • Reset the app data through iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → MorseTap → Delete App

Complete data removal occurs immediately upon app deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

MorseTap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect any personal data from any users, there is no risk of collecting children's information. The app is suitable for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's App Store listing and within the app itself. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store support page for MorseTap.
