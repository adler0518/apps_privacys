CubeMaster Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2026 · Last updated: May 13, 2026

TL;DR — CubeMaster is a fully offline iPhone app. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

1. Introduction

CubeMaster ("we", "our", or "the app") is a 3D Rubik's Cube timer and practice app for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how CubeMaster handles information. By installing or using CubeMaster, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

CubeMaster does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect, receive, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or contact list
  • Your Apple ID or any other account credentials
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Photos, videos, microphone recordings, camera input, or contacts
  • Identifiers for advertising (IDFA), device fingerprints, or IP addresses
  • Analytics, usage telemetry, diagnostics, or crash reports
  • Any form of payment or financial information

The app performs no network requests. It does not include any advertising, analytics, attribution, tracking or remote-configuration SDKs.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

CubeMaster saves a small amount of data locally on your iPhone using Apple's UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Stored dataPurpose
Solve history (completion time, date, move count)Displaying your records and personal best inside the app
App preferences (language, dark mode, haptics, sound)Remembering your settings across launches

Data is removed automatically when you delete the app.

4. Third-Party Services

CubeMaster does not integrate any third-party SDKs, services, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, crash reporters, or back-end servers. There are no third-party data recipients.

5. Permissions

CubeMaster does not request any iOS system permissions (no camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, notifications, Bluetooth, HealthKit, or tracking permission).

6. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your iPhone, it benefits from the built-in protections of iOS, including device-level encryption and Apple's app sandbox. No data is transmitted over the internet by CubeMaster, eliminating the typical network-transit risks.

7. Data Deletion

You can erase all in-app records at any time from the Records screen ("Clear All Records"). Uninstalling CubeMaster from your iPhone permanently removes every piece of data the app ever stored, because nothing is backed up or synced to our side — we have nothing on our side.

8. Children's Privacy

CubeMaster is suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no personal data from anyone, it collects no personal data from children under 13 either. CubeMaster does not knowingly solicit or accept information from children.

9. International Users

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfers. The app behaves identically in every region.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes materially, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of CubeMaster after such changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or CubeMaster, please contact the developer through App Store Connect's Support URL, or reply to the support email shown on the CubeMaster App Store listing.
