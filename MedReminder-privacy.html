Med Reminder Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Med Reminder does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

2. Data Storage

All medication data you enter (name, category, dose, expiry date, notes, user tag) is stored locally in your device's app sandbox as a JSON file. This data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions

The app requests only one optional permission:

  • Local Notifications — used to remind you 7/3/1 days before a medication expires. This permission is requested only when you enable reminders, and can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings.

The app does not request: network, camera, photos, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, health data, or any other sensitive permission.

4. Third Parties

Med Reminder contains no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting tools. There are zero outbound network connections.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no data, no special children's privacy provisions apply.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.

7. Contact

药品保质期管家 隐私政策

生效日期：2026年5月18日

App 不请求：网络、相机、相册、麦克风、位置、通讯录、日历、健康数据或任何其他敏感权限。

4. 第三方

药品保质期管家 不包含任何第三方 SDK、无统计、无广告、无崩溃上报。零网络外连。
