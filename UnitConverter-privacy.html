Privacy Policy Offline App

Unit Converter  |  Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Unit Converter ("the App") is developed by an independent developer. This policy explains how the App handles your information. The short answer: it does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Unit Converter does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Name, email address, or any identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Health or biometric data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data the App creates stays exclusively on your device:

  • Conversion history — JSON file stored in the App's Application Support directory (up to 200 records)
  • Favorites — JSON file in the same directory (unlimited)
  • Preferences — Language selection stored in UserDefaults

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Network Access

Unit Converter makes zero network calls. There are no analytics SDKs, no ad networks, no crash reporters, and no cloud sync. The App works entirely offline.

5. Third-Party Services

Unit Converter does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics libraries, or external services. There are no in-app purchases and no subscriptions.

6. Data Security

Because no data is transmitted off your device, there is no server-side security risk. Your conversion data is as secure as your iOS device itself.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Clearing history from within the App
  • Uninstalling the App (all data is permanently removed)

8. Children's Privacy

Unit Converter does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for use by all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The "Last updated" date at the top of the page will reflect the change date.
