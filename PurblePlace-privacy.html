Privacy Policy for Purble Place

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Purble Place is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. This app is designed for children aged 5-10 and operates 100% offline.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information, usage data, location data, or any other data from users. The app contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no network communication.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores game progress (total stars, cakes baked, memory wins, best times) locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No account creation is required or possible. No personal data is transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services

Purble Place contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising. No internet connection is required or used.

6. Data Deletion

Deleting the app removes all locally stored progress. You can also reset progress from within the Settings screen.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the App Store listing.
