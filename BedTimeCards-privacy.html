BedTimeCards Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

BedTimeCards ("the App") is a fully offline bedtime story app for parents and children. We respect your privacy and have designed the App so that no personal data ever leaves your device.

This Privacy Policy explains exactly what happens (and what does not happen) when you use BedTimeCards.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials (the App has no account system)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, MAC address)
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, microphone, camera
  • Browsing or app usage analytics
  • Crash reports sent to a server
  • Advertising or tracking data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple SwiftData:

  • Reading progress (which stories you have opened, how many times)
  • Favorites you mark on individual story cards
  • Bedtime streak and weekly reading summary
  • App preferences (theme, settings)

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to our servers because we do not operate any server.

4. Third-Party Services

BedTimeCards uses no third-party services. Specifically, the App contains:

  • No analytics SDK (Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising SDK (AdMob, etc.)
  • No social network SDK
  • No cloud storage SDK
  • No payment SDK

5. Data Security

Because data is stored only on your device, security depends on the security of your iPhone. We recommend keeping iOS up to date and using a passcode or Face ID. The App itself does not transmit data over the network.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data created by BedTimeCards, simply uninstall the App. iOS will automatically remove all on-device data associated with the App. You can also reset progress from the in-app Settings screen.

7. Children's Privacy

BedTimeCards is designed for families with children ages 3 to 10. The App does not collect any personal information from children or anyone else. There are no chat features, no user accounts, no public profiles, and no advertising. Parents can use the App with full confidence.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version. Material changes will be communicated through the App Store update notes.
