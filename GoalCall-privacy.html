Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

GoalCall ("we", "our", or "the App") is a FIFA World Cup 2026 score prediction app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use GoalCall.

Summary: GoalCall does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your predictions and scores are stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

GoalCall does not collect any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Account credentials (no account required)

2. Local Data Storage

All data generated within GoalCall (match predictions, scores, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never transmitted to any external server.

3. Photo Library Access

GoalCall requests permission to save images to your Photo Library only when you choose to save a share card. This permission is optional and only used for saving generated images. We do not access, read, or upload any existing photos from your device.

4. Network Requests

GoalCall connects to football-data.org to fetch live match schedules and scores. These requests contain no personal information. The App also works fully offline with built-in match data.

5. Third-Party Services

GoalCall does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs collecting data in the background.

6. Children's Privacy

GoalCall does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all GoalCall data by simply uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App and on this page with an updated effective date.
