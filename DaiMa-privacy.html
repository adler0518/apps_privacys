DaiMa (袋马) Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

"DaiMa" (袋马, the "App") is operated by Alibaba (China) Co., Ltd and its affiliates. We respect and protect user privacy. This policy explains how the App collects, uses and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device & runtime info: OS version, device model, crash logs, performance metrics — used for stability and troubleshooting.
  • Prompts & generated content: Your text input and generated mini-app snippets are sent to Alibaba Cloud LLM for generation; they are not used for model training and not shared with other users.
  • Microphone: Activated only when you tap "voice input"; speech is transcribed to text and sent to the AI. No background recording.
  • Camera & photo library: Activated only when you choose to attach an image; the selected image is used only for the current AI session.
  • Account: Core features do not require sign-up. If you sign in, only an opaque user identifier is stored to sync your works.

2. How We Use Information

  • Deliver core features: prompt-to-app generation, preview, "My Apps" management.
  • Improve product quality with aggregated, non-identifiable statistics.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required.

3. Third-party Sharing

Except for the Alibaba Cloud LLM service required for AI generation, we do not sell or share your prompts or uploaded images with any third party.

4. Storage & Security

"My Apps" content is stored on-device by default. Cloud requests use HTTPS; the server does not retain identifiable prompt content for long-term storage.

5. Your Rights

You can delete local data in "My Apps" anytime, or revoke microphone/camera/photo permissions via system Settings → Privacy & Security.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13.
