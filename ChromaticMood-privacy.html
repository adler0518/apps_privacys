Chromatic Mood - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Data Collection

Chromatic Mood does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All mood color records and preferences are stored exclusively on your device using Apple SwiftData.

Network Usage

Chromatic Mood does not make any network requests. The app operates entirely offline. No data is ever sent to any server.

Permissions

  • Notifications: Used solely for optional daily recording reminders. You can enable or disable them at any time in Settings. No notification data is shared.
  • Photo Library (Write-only): Used only when you choose to export your spectrum painting to the Photos app. We never read or access your existing photos.

Data Storage

All data is stored in the app's sandbox on your device and is included in standard iCloud device backups. You can export all records as JSON or delete all data at any time through Settings > Data Management.

Third-Party Services

Chromatic Mood does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Chromatic Mood does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
