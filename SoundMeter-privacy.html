Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2026

SoundMeter ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use SoundMeter.

Information We Do Not Collect

SoundMeter does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies
  • We do not have user accounts or registration
  • We do not transmit any data over the internet

Microphone Access

SoundMeter requires access to your device's microphone solely to measure ambient sound levels in real time. This is essential for the App's core functionality as a decibel meter.

Important:

  • Audio from the microphone is processed entirely on your device in real time
  • No audio is recorded, stored, or saved to files
  • No audio data is transmitted to any server or third party
  • Audio processing stops immediately when you stop a measurement or close the App
  • Only numerical sound level values (decibel readings) are derived from the audio and saved locally as measurement records

Local Data Storage

Measurement history (decibel readings, timestamps, and labels) is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any cloud service
  • Can be deleted at any time through the App's settings
  • Is removed when you uninstall the App

Third-Party Services

SoundMeter does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services.

Children's Privacy

SoundMeter does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
