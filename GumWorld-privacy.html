GumWorld Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

1. Overview

GumWorld ("we", "our", "us") is a knowledge encyclopedia app about chewing gum. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. GumWorld is a fully offline app that does not require network access, user accounts, or any form of personal information.

3. Permissions

GumWorld does not request any device permissions such as camera, location, contacts, or microphone.

4. Third-Party Services

GumWorld does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Storage

All app content is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

GumWorld is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update.
