FlashCard Go - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Overview

FlashCard Go is designed with your privacy as a top priority. The app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. FlashCard Go does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Data Storage

All your flashcards, decks, learning progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

Notifications (Optional): If you choose to enable daily study reminders, the app will request permission to send local notifications. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication.

No other permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.) are requested or used.

Third-Party Services

FlashCard Go does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

FlashCard Go does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through Settings > Delete All Data within the app. Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date above.
