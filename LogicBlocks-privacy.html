Information We Collect

Logic Blocks does not collect any personal information. All puzzle progress, scores, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using UserDefaults. No data is ever sent to external servers.

Data Storage

All app data (puzzle completion records, best move counts, best times, star ratings, settings) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data. Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. There are no network requests of any kind made by the app.

Children's Privacy

Logic Blocks does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is fully offline and collects no data from any user of any age.
