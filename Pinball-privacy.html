Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Introduction

Pinball ("the App") is a physics-based arcade game developed by the AI Team. This policy explains how the App handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

Information We Do Not Collect

Pinball does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login required
  • No name, email, or contact information collected
  • No location data accessed
  • No device identifiers tracked
  • No analytics or usage data sent to any server
  • No advertising identifiers used

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores gameplay data locally on your device only:

  • High score and rank history
  • Lifetime statistics (games played, bumper hits, missions completed)
  • Game settings

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults and never leaves your device.

Network Access

Pinball requires no network connection. The App does not make any network requests. There is no server-side component.

Third-Party Services

Pinball does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

Pinball does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for all ages.

Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, there is no risk of data breach from our side. All local data is protected by iOS standard security.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data. No action is required on your part.
