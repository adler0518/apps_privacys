Privacy Policy

Bulb Keeper  ·  Effective date: May 14, 2026

100% Offline App

1. Introduction

Bulb Keeper ("the App") is developed by AITeam Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. In short: it doesn't collect any.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any personal data, including:

  • Name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Your bulb inventory (room names, fixture descriptions, bulb specifications) is saved locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are zero network requests made by the App.

5. Data Security

All data resides solely on your device under the protection of iOS's standard sandboxing and encryption mechanisms. Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all associated data.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall Bulb Keeper from your device. No additional steps are needed, and no data persists on any remote server.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from users of any age. It is safe to use for all age groups.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
