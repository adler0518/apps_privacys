Block Drop

Privacy Policy · Last updated: May 14, 2026

Block Drop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected by design.

1. Introduction

Block Drop is a cyberpunk-themed Tetromino puzzle game for iPhone. Because Block Drop operates entirely offline and collects no user data, this privacy policy is straightforward: there is nothing to disclose.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or any personal identifier
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • Usage statistics or analytics events
  • Photos, contacts, or media library
  • Financial or payment information
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to external servers

3. Data Stored On Your Device

Block Drop saves only your high score locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us or any third party, and is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Block Drop integrates no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social login providers. No third parties have access to any information about your use of this app.

5. Internet Access

Block Drop does not require or use an internet connection. No data is transmitted over any network at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Block Drop is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app complies fully with COPPA and similar regulations worldwide.

7. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected externally, there is no personal data at risk. Your game progress is protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with Block Drop, simply uninstall the app. All locally stored data is immediately and permanently removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we change our data practices, we will update this Privacy Policy and revise the "Last updated" date.
