Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Killer Sudoku ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in our Killer Sudoku iOS application.

Important: Our app is designed to be 100% offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent: Killer Sudoku does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, contacts, or any personal files
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Health or fitness data
Zero Data Collection: Our app operates entirely offline. No network requests are made, no data leaves your device, and no third-party services have access to your information.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Killer Sudoku stores the following data locally on your iOS device using Apple's UserDefaults system:

  • Game Progress: Your current puzzle state (filled cells, notes, timer)
  • Best Times: Your fastest completion times for each difficulty level
  • Completed Games Count: Number of puzzles completed per difficulty

This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Accessible only to the Killer Sudoku app (iOS sandbox protection)

4. Third-Party Services

Killer Sudoku does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

The app contains:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services (such as Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services
  • No tracking or attribution services

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, there is no risk of data interception during transmission or server-side breaches.

iOS provides built-in security through:

  • App Sandbox: Each app's data is isolated and protected
  • Device Encryption: iOS devices encrypt data at rest
  • Access Control: Only your app can access its UserDefaults data

6. Data Deletion

All local game data is automatically and permanently deleted when you:

  • Uninstall the Killer Sudoku app from your device
  • Erase all content and settings on your iOS device

No data recovery is possible after deletion, as we maintain no servers or backups.

7. Children's Privacy

Killer Sudoku is appropriate for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 or any other age group.

Parents can be confident that:

  • No personal data is collected from their children
  • No behavioral advertising is present
  • No third-party tracking occurs
  • The app is completely safe for offline puzzle solving

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
