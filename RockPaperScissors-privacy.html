Privacy Policy

App: RPS Cyber  |  Bundle ID: com.xiadu.rockpaperscissors  |  Last updated: May 20, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RPS Cyber ("the App") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. RPS Cyber does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, device ID)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third-party services
  • Browsing history or search queries

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Match history (last 50 rounds) — stored in Application Support as JSON
  • Win streak and best streak counters — stored in Application Support as JSON
  • User preferences (difficulty, voice language, sound toggle) — stored in UserDefaults

All data remains entirely on your device. It is never uploaded, synced, or backed up to any server. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

RPS Cyber does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social frameworks. The App operates entirely offline with no network requests.

5. Permissions

RPS Cyber does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, health data, or any other sensitive device permission. The App uses the device's built-in speech synthesizer (AVSpeechSynthesizer) for voice announcements — this runs entirely on-device and requires no special permission.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit your data, there is no server-side risk. Local data is stored in the App's sandboxed container, accessible only to RPS Cyber.

7. Data Deletion

You can clear all game history and statistics at any time from within the App (Settings → Reset All Data). Uninstalling the App also permanently deletes all locally stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

RPS Cyber does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: adler0518@icloud.com
