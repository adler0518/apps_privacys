DominoClassic Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Overview

DominoClassic ("the App") is a single-player domino board game. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the App does and does not collect.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store or transmit any personal information. There is no account system, no sign-up, no login, and no analytics SDK embedded in the App.

3. Local Data

Game progress, statistics, settings (difficulty, rule variant, haptics) and recent match history are stored only on your device using the standard iOS UserDefaults mechanism. This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App removes all of it.

4. Permissions

The App does not request access to the camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, calendar, motion sensors, Bluetooth or any other sensitive permission. The App uses light haptic feedback through the standard system haptics API, which does not require permission.

5. Network

The App is fully offline. It does not contain advertising networks, third-party trackers or remote configuration services. No outbound network connections are initiated by the App.

6. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

7. Changes

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at the same URL with an updated date.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact: support@example.com
