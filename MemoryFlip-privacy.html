Privacy Policy

MemoryFlip · Last updated: May 17, 2026
Summary. MemoryFlip is a fully offline memory card-matching game. We do not collect, transmit or store any personal data on any server. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the iOS application MemoryFlip ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account information.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera data.
  • We do not collect any device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising ID).
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device:

  • Your selected difficulty and card theme.
  • Your sound and haptic-feedback preferences.
  • Your selected appearance (light / dark / system) and language preference.
  • Your game history (theme, difficulty, time, moves, score and date for each session — up to 100 most recent entries).
  • Your best score per (theme × difficulty) combination.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. No data is shared with anyone.

5. Network Access

The App does not require an internet connection to function. The App does not make any network requests to any server.

6. Data Security

All settings and gameplay history are stored locally inside the App's sandboxed storage on your device, protected by standard iOS application sandboxing.

7. Data Deletion

You can clear all gameplay history at any time via Records → Clear All inside the App. Uninstalling the App deletes all locally stored data immediately and permanently.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is appropriate for all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, no special handling of children's data is required.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
