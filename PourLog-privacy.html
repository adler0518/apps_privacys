PourLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

PourLog is a resin dice recipe tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No network requests

3. Data Stored on Your Device

PourLog stores recipe cards, photos, tags, and timer settings locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

PourLog does not integrate any third-party services. The app functions entirely offline.

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData with device-level encryption.

6. Data Deletion

Delete the app to remove all data.

7. Children's Privacy

PourLog does not collect data from any user.
