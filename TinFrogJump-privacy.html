Privacy Policy

Tin Frog Jump   Version 1.0

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tin Frog Jump ("the App", "we") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Tin Frog Jump is a fully offline game. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

The App makes zero network connections. No data leaves your device.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally using Apple's UserDefaults API. This data never leaves your device:

  • Best Distance — your all-time highest score
  • Last Distance — score from your most recent game session
  • Total Jumps — cumulative jump count across all sessions

You can delete all this data at any time by deleting the App from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are no third-party libraries beyond Apple's standard iOS frameworks (SwiftUI, UIKit, Combine).

5. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's built-in sandboxing and security mechanisms. We have no access to this data.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with Tin Frog Jump, simply delete the App from your iPhone. The Settings screen within the App also provides a "Reset Data" option to clear all local records.

7. Children's Privacy

Tin Frog Jump does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be published here with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
