Privacy Policy

Music Box Retro  ·  Last updated: 2026-05-20

1. Introduction

Music Box Retro ("the App") is a cyberpunk-themed virtual music box that plays classic melodies synthesized as bell tones on your device. This policy explains how the App handles your information. The short answer: it doesn't collect any.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Music Box Retro does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email, or contact details
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts or calendar data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores two small values in your device's local UserDefaults (App Preferences):

  • Total play count — used to determine which melodies you have unlocked
  • Selected melody ID — remembers your last chosen tune
  • Playback speed setting — remembers your preferred speed (0.5x–2.0x)
  • Haptics preference — on/off toggle

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. No Network Access

Music Box Retro makes zero network requests. It has no internet permission, connects to no servers, and cannot transmit any data even if it wanted to.

5. Third-Party Services

The App contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or cloud services of any kind.

6. Data Deletion

All App data is stored exclusively on your device. To delete it completely, uninstall the App from your iPhone. No account deletion is required because no account exists.

7. Children's Privacy

Music Box Retro does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13. The App is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and release a new App version. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
