Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

Introduction

Stroop Sprint ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Stroop Sprint does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data (name, email, phone number) is collected
  • No location data is accessed
  • No camera, microphone, or photo library access is used
  • No analytics or tracking tools are integrated
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data are included
  • No network requests are made — the app works entirely offline

Local Data Storage

Game statistics (scores, accuracy, reaction times) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Stroop Sprint does not use any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
