Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Stroop Sprint ("we", "our", or "the App") is a cognitive challenge game that operates entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Stroop Sprint does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial information
  • Photos, videos, or media

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Game settings (mode, difficulty, duration preferences)
  • Best scores and accuracy statistics
  • Language preference (English/Chinese)
  • Haptic feedback preference

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Stroop Sprint does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social media frameworks. The App functions completely offline.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is managed by iOS system security, your information is protected by Apple's built-in device encryption and security measures.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all App data, simply uninstall Stroop Sprint from your device. All locally stored preferences and statistics will be permanently removed. You can also reset individual settings within the App.

7. Children's Privacy

Stroop Sprint does not collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us
