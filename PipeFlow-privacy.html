Privacy Policy

Pipe Flow · Last updated: May 14, 2026
100% Offline · No Data Collected
Short version: Pipe Flow runs entirely on your device. It does not connect to the internet, does not create accounts, does not ask for any system permission, and does not collect any personal data. Uninstalling the app removes everything.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pipe Flow iOS application ("Pipe Flow", "the app", "we", "us") handles information. By installing or using the app you agree to the practices described below.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, sell, share, or monetize any user data. Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising IDs)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, camera feed, microphone audio
  • Health, financial, or sensitive information
  • Browsing history, search history, usage analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic logs sent to any remote server

3. Data Stored Only on Your Device

The following data is saved locally via iOS UserDefaults and never leaves your device:

  • Highest unlocked level and total number of levels cleared
  • Best completion time per level
  • Total rotation counter (gameplay statistic)
  • Your toggles for sound, haptics, and flow highlight

This data is used solely to render the app UI and resume your game. It is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Pipe Flow contains no third-party SDKs. It does not integrate analytics, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or cloud backends of any kind.

5. System Permissions

The app does not request any iOS system permission. No camera, microphone, photo library, location, contacts, motion, notification, or Bluetooth access is ever requested.

6. Network Usage

Pipe Flow does not make any network request. The app works fully in airplane mode. You can block the app from accessing the network at the OS level without any impact on functionality.

7. Data Security

Because no data ever leaves your device, there is no transmission to secure. Locally stored preferences and progress are protected by iOS sandboxing and the built-in Data Protection features of your device.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all Pipe Flow data, simply delete the app from your device. iOS will remove the app's sandbox, which erases every saved preference, level progress record, and best-time entry.

9. Children's Privacy

Pipe Flow is safe for all ages. It does not collect any personal information, including from children. There are no in-app purchases, no external links, no chat, and no advertising.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Email pipeflow.support@icloud.com.
