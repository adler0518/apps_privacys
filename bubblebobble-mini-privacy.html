Privacy Policy

Summary

We built Bubble Bobble Mini as a completely offline, privacy-first arcade game. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

Data Collection

  • We do NOT collect any personal information.
  • We do NOT use analytics or crash-reporting services.

  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies.

Data Storage

Game progress (unlocked levels, scores, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Network Access

This application makes zero network requests. It operates entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
