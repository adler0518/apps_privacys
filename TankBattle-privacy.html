Tank Battle — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-05-13

Overview

Tank Battle is a 100% offline single-player game. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults API.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs (no ATT, no IDFA)
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reports sent to third parties
  • No in-app purchases or payment information

Local Storage

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device, never transmitted anywhere:

  • Per-level best scores (integer values via UserDefaults)
  • Level unlock progress

Permissions

Tank Battle requests zero system permissions. No access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other device resource is requested.

No Location No Camera No Microphone No Contacts No IDFA

Third-Party Services

Tank Battle uses no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or cloud services. All gameplay is rendered locally using Apple's SpriteKit framework.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Data Retention

Local game data (scores, progress) remains on your device until you delete the app. Deleting the app permanently removes all associated data.
