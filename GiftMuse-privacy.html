Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026 · App: GiftMuse

1. Introduction

GiftMuse ("we", "our", or "the App") is a gift recommendation utility that runs entirely on your device. We respect your privacy and believe great apps should not need to track you. This policy explains exactly what data the App handles and what it does not.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

GiftMuse does not collect, transmit, sell, share or otherwise process any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, contact list or address book
  • Your location, IP address, advertising identifier (IDFA) or device identifiers
  • Your photos, microphone, camera or any media on your device
  • Analytics, usage statistics, crash data or behavioral telemetry
  • Any data through cookies or tracking pixels

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores a small amount of data locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite gift ideas (saved with a tap of the heart button)
  • Your most recent input selections (relationship, occasion, budget, interests) for convenience
  • App settings such as language preference

This data never leaves your device and is not synced to any server, cloud or third party.

4. Third-Party Services

GiftMuse does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks or social-media APIs. There are no third parties involved in any way.

5. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted off it, your information is protected by the standard sandboxing and encryption of iOS.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data created by GiftMuse, simply uninstall the App from your device. Deleting the App removes all of its local data immediately and irreversibly. There is no server-side data to delete because we don't have any servers.

7. Children's Privacy

GiftMuse is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
