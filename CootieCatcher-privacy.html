Cootie Catcher

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cootie Catcher. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices. The summary: we collect nothing.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, Apple ID)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics data
  • Game choices or play history content
  • Payment or financial information

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Cootie Catcher stores the following data exclusively on your device via Apple's UserDefaults. It never leaves your device:

  • Play history: records of past game rounds (category and topic phrase only)
  • Daily Pick status: whether today's daily topic card has been revealed
  • App settings: language and haptic feedback preferences

Deleting the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

4. Network Usage

Cootie Catcher is 100% offline. No network requests are ever made. No data is transmitted to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

Cootie Catcher uses no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, crash reporters, or advertising networks.

6. Permissions Required

Cootie Catcher requires no special device permissions. No camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library access is needed.

7. Children's Privacy

Cootie Catcher collects no data from any user, making it inherently compliant with COPPA and similar regulations for users of all ages.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all Cootie Catcher data, simply delete the app from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will reflect in the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app implies acceptance.
