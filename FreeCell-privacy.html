Privacy Policy for FreeCell

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

FreeCell ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the FreeCell mobile application.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

FreeCell operates entirely offline. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or device identifiers

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores game statistics (games played, win rate, streaks, best time) locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

FreeCell contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The App makes no network requests whatsoever.

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach through our App. All game data remains on your device.

6. Data Deletion

Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all locally stored game statistics. No additional action is required.

7. Children's Privacy

FreeCell does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the App Store listing and within the App.
