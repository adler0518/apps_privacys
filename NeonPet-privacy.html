Privacy Policy for Neon Pet

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Neon Pet (霓虹宠物) is committed to your privacy. This app operates 100% offline.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit: personal information, usage analytics, location data, device identifiers, or any other data.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Pet state (species, stats, coins, life stage) is saved locally in a JSON file using FileManager. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Neon Pet contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks. The App makes no network requests.

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of external data breach.

6. Data Deletion

Deleting the App removes all locally stored pet data. No additional action required.

7. Children's Privacy

Neon Pet does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be reflected in the App Store listing and within the App.
