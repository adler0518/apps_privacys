Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

Overview

TireWiki ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. TireWiki is a fully offline application. All content (tire knowledge, specifications, guides) is stored locally on your device and does not require an internet connection to function.

Data Storage

All app content is bundled within the application at build time. No user data is collected, processed, or stored on any external servers. The app does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Third-Party Services

TireWiki does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks included in the application.

Children's Privacy

TireWiki does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not contain any content that would be inappropriate for minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing for TireWiki.
