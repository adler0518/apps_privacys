Privacy Policy - TireWiki

Last Updated: June 2026

TireWiki ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection

TireWiki does NOT collect any personal data.

  • No account registration required
  • No personal information collected
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No location data accessed
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts accessed
  • No data shared with third parties

Data Storage

All content in TireWiki is built into the application and stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server or cloud service.

Third-Party Services

TireWiki does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising frameworks. The app functions entirely offline.

Data Deletion

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to delete. Simply uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device.

Children's Privacy

TireWiki does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.
