Word Chain Master — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

1. Summary

Word Chain Master is a fully offline Chinese idiom chain game. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. Period.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account credentials
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) used for tracking
  • No analytics, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs

3. Local Data Storage

Game preferences (language, sound settings) are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time via the iOS Settings app or by reinstalling.

4. Network Access

Word Chain Master does not include any network entitlements. The app does not connect to any server, CDN, or remote service.

5. Children's Privacy

Word Chain Master is an educational app suitable for all ages. Since we collect no data, the protection requirements under COPPA and similar regulations are inherently satisfied.

6. Permissions in Info.plist

Word Chain Master declares zero NSUsageDescription keys. The app cannot and does not request access to any sensitive iOS resource.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top of this page will be revised. As long as the app remains fully offline, the substance will not change.

8. Contact

For questions, email: 544627835@qq.com

成语接龙大师 — 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026-05-18

1. 摘要

成语接龙大师是一款完全离线的中华成语接龙游戏。我们不收集、不存储、不共享、不传输任何个人数据。仅此而已。

成语接龙大师不包含任何网络权限声明。应用不连接任何服务器、CDN 或远程服务。

5. 儿童隐私

6. Info.plist 权限声明

成语接龙大师未声明任何 NSUsageDescription 键，因此无法且不会请求访问任何 iOS 敏感资源。
