Privacy Policy

App Name: Mood Diary / 心情日记
Bundle ID: com.xiadu.moodweatherdiary
Last Updated: May 2026

This app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information We Collect

Mood Diary does not collect any personal information. All diary entries, mood records, weather logs, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local file storage. No data is ever sent to external servers.

Data Storage

All app data (mood entries, weather records, notes, streak counts, app settings) is stored locally on your device in a JSON file within the app's Documents directory. We have no access to this data. Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no network requests of any kind made by the app.

Children's Privacy

Mood Diary does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is fully offline and collects no personal data from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
