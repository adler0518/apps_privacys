DialForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

DialForge ("the App") is a watch dial design tool developed by Xiadu. We respect your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. DialForge is a fully offline application. It does not require an account, does not connect to any server to collect data, and does not transmit any of your information to us or any third party.

How Your Data Is Stored

All content you create in DialForge — including your dial projects, templates, tick mark settings, text elements, layers, and exported designs — is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data. Settings are saved on-device using standard system storage.

Permissions

DialForge does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, or any other protected resource. When you export a design, the file is saved through the system's standard share sheet, which you control.

Third-Party Services

DialForge does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs. No usage data is shared with anyone.

Children's Privacy

DialForge does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your designs stay private and under your control. Deleting the App removes all associated local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
