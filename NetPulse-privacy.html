Privacy Policy for NetPulse

Last updated: 2026-05-13

1. Introduction

NetPulse ("the App") is an iOS utility that measures your network speed locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App does and does not handle.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or account
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Your contacts, photos, files, or health data
  • Any advertising identifiers (IDFA)

3. Data Stored on Your Device

To show your speed test history and trends, the App stores the following locally on your device, in the App sandbox:

  • Timestamps of each speed test (ISO-8601)
  • Measured download / upload speed, ping, jitter
  • The detected network type (Wi-Fi / 5G / 4G / Cellular)
  • Your theme / retention policy / language preference

This data never leaves your device. It is deleted when you delete the App or manually clear history.

4. Third Party Services

The App does not integrate any third party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. Speed tests are performed against public CDN endpoints purely to measure throughput; no personal data is sent.

5. Data Security

All locally stored data lives in the App sandbox and is protected by iOS Data Protection. No server component exists, so there is no cloud attack surface.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all history at any time from Settings, Data Retention, or by uninstalling the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is safe for all audiences. Since no data is collected or transmitted, it complies with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this Policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and the updated version will be available at the same URL.
