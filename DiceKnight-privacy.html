Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Dice Knight (骰子骑士) mobile application ("App"), developed and published by an independent developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Introduction

Dice Knight is a turn-based dice strategy RPG game for iPhone. The App is designed to operate entirely offline and does not require any network connection to function. Your privacy is fundamentally protected by design.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information whatsoever.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device content
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history or search queries

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Game progress: Current chapter, level, and battle state
  • Settings preferences: Sound and music on/off toggles

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Dice Knight does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. The App contains:

  • No ads
  • No analytics SDKs (no Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • No social media SDKs
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No in-app purchase frameworks

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and no network communication occurs, your data is protected by the security measures built into iOS, including:

  • iOS sandboxing (app data isolation)
  • Device encryption (when passcode is enabled)
  • No network attack surface (the App makes zero network requests)

6. Data Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data associated with Dice Knight:

  • Delete the App from your device — this permanently removes all associated data
  • There is no server-side data to request deletion for

7. Children's Privacy

Dice Knight does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). The App is safe for users of all ages as it operates entirely offline with no data collection.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since the App does not collect contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically.
