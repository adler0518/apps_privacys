❓ FAQ / 常见问题

Q: The game has no sound on my iPhone. / 游戏没有声音

A: Please check if your device is in Silent Mode (the switch on the side of your iPhone). The game audio will play regardless of the silent switch in the latest version. If you're using an older version, please update to the latest. / 请检查设备是否处于静音模式。最新版本已修复此问题，请更新到最新版。

Q: How do I save my progress? / 如何保存游戏进度？

A: The game automatically saves your progress after each battle. / 游戏会在每场战斗后自动保存进度。

Q: How do I change the language? / 如何切换语言？

A: Go to Settings in the game and select your preferred language (Chinese/English/Auto). / 进入游戏设置，选择你偏好的语言（中文/英文/跟随系统）。

Q: Does the game require an internet connection? / 游戏需要联网吗？

A: No. DiceKnight is a fully offline game. No internet connection is required. / 不需要。骰子骑士是完全离线的游戏，无需网络连接。

Q: Does the game collect my personal data? / 游戏会收集我的个人数据吗？

A: No. We do not collect any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details. / 不会。我们不收集任何个人数据。详情请查看隐私政策。
