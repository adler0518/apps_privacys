Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my heart rate not changing during practice?

Please ensure your Apple Watch is worn snugly with the heart rate sensor in contact with your skin. Heart rate changes after exercise may take longer to reflect breathing effects.

Can I do other activities while practicing?

We recommend staying in a quiet sitting or lying position, avoiding talking or moving, to get the best heart rate feedback.

How many times per day should I practice?

We suggest 2-3 sessions per day, each lasting 3-8 minutes. You can practice anytime you feel anxious or have trouble sleeping.

Why can't I connect my Apple Watch?

Please check: (1) Bluetooth is enabled, (2) Apple Watch has sufficient battery and is powered on, (3) Health permissions are granted. You can also try restarting both devices.

Is breathing exercise safe?

Breathing exercises are generally safe for most people. However, if you have respiratory conditions, heart conditions, or other health concerns, please consult your healthcare provider before using this app.
