1. 概述

感谢您使用「找茬大师」（以下简称「本应用」）。我们高度重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在以清晰的方式说明本应用如何处理与您相关的信息。本应用是一款单机休闲游戏，不收集、不上传、不分享任何个人信息。

2. 我们不收集的信息

本应用不会收集以下任何信息：

  • 姓名、电话、邮箱、身份证、住址等个人身份信息
  • 位置信息（GPS、IP 定位、Wi-Fi/蓝牙周边）
  • 通讯录、相册、相机、麦克风、健康数据
  • 设备唯一标识符（IDFA、IDFV、广告标识等）

  • 支付与账户信息（本应用为完全免费，无内购、无广告）

3. 数据的本地存储

为了保存您的游戏进度，本应用会在您的设备本地存储少量数据，包括：

  • 关卡完成状态、最佳通关用时、星级评价
  • 音效与界面偏好设置

上述数据仅保存在您本机的应用沙盒中（通过 iOS 系统的 UserDefaults 等本地机制），不会上传到任何服务器。卸载本应用时，所有本地数据将随应用一并删除。

4. 系统权限

本应用不申请任何系统权限，包括但不限于位置、相机、麦克风、相册、通讯录、推送通知等。

5. 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方 SDK，包括广告 SDK、统计分析 SDK、推送 SDK、社交分享 SDK 等。所有功能均在本机离线完成。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用适合所有年龄段使用，且不收集任何用户信息，因此天然符合儿童隐私保护相关规定（包括但不限于 COPPA、GDPR-K）。家长可放心让儿童使用。

7. 数据安全

由于本应用不收集任何信息，也无任何网络传输，您的隐私数据不存在泄露风险。所有游戏进度仅保留在您的设备本地，由 iOS 系统沙盒机制提供安全保护。

8. 政策变更

若本隐私政策发生重大变更，我们将在新版应用中更新本页面，并通过版本说明告知您。

9. 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问、意见或建议，欢迎通过 App Store 应用页面的开发者反馈渠道与我们联系。

Privacy Policy — Spot Master

App Name: Spot Master (找茬大师)　·　Bundle ID: com.zyl04401.spotdiff20260517
Effective Date: 2026-05-15　·　Last Updated: 2026-05-15

1. Overview

Thank you for using Spot Master (hereinafter, "the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains, in plain language, how the App handles information related to you. The App is a single-player offline puzzle game. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers such as name, phone number, email, ID, or address
  • Location data (GPS, IP, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth scans)
  • Contacts, Photos, Camera, Microphone, or Health data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising IDs)
  • Behavioral data, click logs, or crash reports (no analytics SDK is integrated)
  • Payment or account information (the App is fully free, with no In-App Purchase and no ads)

3. Local Data Storage

To save your game progress, the App stores a small amount of data locally on your device, including:

  • Level completion status, best clear time, and star rating
  • Sound and UI preferences

Such data is kept exclusively within the App's local sandbox via iOS system facilities such as UserDefaults, and is never uploaded to any server. Uninstalling the App removes all local data automatically.

4. System Permissions

The App requests NO system permissions, including but not limited to Location, Camera, Microphone, Photos, Contacts, or Push Notifications.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates NO third-party SDKs, including no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, push SDKs, or social sharing SDKs. All features run entirely offline on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any user information. It therefore inherently complies with applicable children's privacy regulations, including COPPA and GDPR-K. Parents may allow their children to use the App with confidence.

7. Data Security

Because the App collects no information and performs no network transmission, there is no risk of data leakage. All progress remains on your device, protected by the iOS sandbox.

8. Policy Changes

If this Privacy Policy is materially changed, the updated version will be published in a new release of the App and noted in release notes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the developer feedback channel on the App Store product page.
