Klondike – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Klondike ("the App") is a classic solitaire card game developed for iPhone. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Klondike does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Name, email, or account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Any data that leaves your device

3. Data Stored On Your Device

All game data (current game state, statistics, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using iOS native storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Network Access

Klondike does not request any network permissions. The App operates 100% offline. No internet connection is required or used at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Klondike does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or social login services.

6. Children's Privacy

Klondike is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data by uninstalling the App. No additional steps are required.
