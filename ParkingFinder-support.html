Quick Start Guide

Save your parking spot

  1. Open ParkTrack after you park.
  2. Tap the "Save Parking" button on the main screen.
  3. (Optional) Take a photo of the spot or add a note to help you remember the level, row, or nearby landmarks.

Find your car

  1. Open the app and tap the active parking record.
  2. Switch to the compass view — the arrow points to your car and shows the straight-line distance.
  3. Or use the map view to see both your position and the parked location.

Delete a record

  1. Go to the Records list.
  2. Swipe left on any record, or open the detail sheet and tap Delete.

Frequently Asked Questions

The compass arrow seems to point in the wrong direction. What should I do?

The compass relies on your iPhone's built-in magnetometer. Move away from metal objects, car bodies, and strong magnetic fields, then slowly rotate the phone in a figure-8 pattern to recalibrate. Also make sure Location Services and Compass Calibration are enabled in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

Why does my parking location look inaccurate on the map?

GPS accuracy can drop in underground garages, indoor parking lots, or areas with tall buildings. For best results, save the spot while you are still close to an open-sky area, or add a photo and a note (e.g. "B2, Row 14") to supplement the GPS coordinate.

The app is not asking for location permission, or location is not working.

Go to iOS Settings → ParkTrack → Location, and set it to "While Using the App". Location access is required for the core feature (saving and navigating back to your parking spot).

Can I use ParkTrack without granting camera or photo library access?

Yes. Camera and photo library are optional. If you decline them, you can still save parking records with GPS coordinates and text notes only.

Is my parking data uploaded to a server?

No. All parking records, photos, and preferences are stored locally on your iPhone only. Nothing is uploaded. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I uninstalled the app — can I recover my records?

Because data is stored only on your device, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local records. There is currently no cloud backup.

Does ParkTrack support iPad?

ParkTrack is designed primarily for iPhone. On iPad it runs in iPhone compatibility mode. The core features (save parking spot, compass, map) still work, but the layout is optimized for iPhone.

Is ParkTrack free?

Yes. ParkTrack is free to use and contains no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.
