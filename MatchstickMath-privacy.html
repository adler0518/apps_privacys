Privacy Policy

App: MatchstickMath  |  Last Updated: May 2026

Overview

MatchstickMath is a fully offline matchstick puzzle game. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. This policy explains what data (if any) exists on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

MatchstickMath does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, Apple ID)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, IP address)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Contacts, messages, photos, or media

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores only:

  • Puzzle progress — which levels you have completed
  • Settings preferences — haptics on/off, visual preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

Network Access

MatchstickMath does not require or request any network permissions. The app functions entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

MatchstickMath does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social frameworks.

Children's Privacy

MatchstickMath is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no special concerns under COPPA or similar regulations.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no data security risk from our systems. Local device data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall MatchstickMath from your device. No server-side data exists to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
