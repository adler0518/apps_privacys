ClearSpace Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Summary: ClearSpace does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device. No data ever leaves your iPhone.

Our Commitment to Privacy

ClearSpace ("the App") is developed by Xiadu ("we", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data We Collect

We collect no data. ClearSpace is designed with a privacy-first architecture:

  • No account required — No sign-up, no login, no email address needed.
  • No network requests — The App makes zero internet connections. All operations are performed locally on your device.
  • No analytics or tracking — No usage data, crash reports, or behavioral tracking of any kind.
  • No third-party SDKs — No advertising, analytics, or data collection frameworks are included.

How Your Data Is Used

ClearSpace requires access to certain device features to perform its core functions. This data is processed locally and never transmitted:

  • Photo Library — Used to scan for duplicate photos, screenshots, and large files. Photos are analyzed on-device using Apple's Vision framework and perceptual hashing. No photos are uploaded anywhere.
  • Contacts — Used to detect and merge duplicate contact entries. Contact data is processed entirely on-device.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect or transmit any data. Your photos, contacts, and usage information remain exclusively on your device.

Data Retention

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to retain on any server. The only data stored is your app preferences and cleanup history, saved locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • You can revoke Photo Library or Contacts access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the App and all associated local data by uninstalling it from your device.
  • No data is stored remotely, so there is nothing to request deletion of from any server.

Children's Privacy

ClearSpace does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation or transmit any data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
