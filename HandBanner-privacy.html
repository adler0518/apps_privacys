Privacy Policy — HandBanner

Effective Date: May 17, 2026 · Last Updated: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

HandBanner (the "App") is a handheld LED banner / marquee app for iPhone, developed and distributed for personal and event use. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles and how it is stored.

HandBanner is a 100% offline app. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us or any third party.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, account ID)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising identifiers)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Health, fitness, or financial data
  • Any data sent over the network

The App does not request any iOS permissions and does not require an internet connection to function.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

To remember your last banner setup, the App stores the following only on your device, in standard iOS app preferences (UserDefaults):

  • Your last entered banner text
  • Your chosen text and background colors
  • Your chosen font, size, scroll speed, scroll direction
  • Your blink, glow and decoration preferences
  • Your preferred UI language

This information never leaves your device. We have no servers and no way to read it.

4. Third-Party Services

HandBanner does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are no external services involved in the App's operation.

5. Data Security

All settings are stored within the App's sandboxed container, protected by standard iOS file system encryption while your device is locked. Because no data is transmitted off-device, network-based data breaches are not applicable.

6. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all data the App has stored by either:

  • Tapping Reset to Default inside the App's settings, or
  • Uninstalling HandBanner from your iPhone, which removes the entire app container along with all stored preferences.

7. Children's Privacy

HandBanner is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App does not contain advertising or in-app purchases.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at the URL where you are reading this document. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
