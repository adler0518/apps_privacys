Privacy Policy

Bridge Guard (守桥大作战)  ·  Effective June 18, 2026

Bridge Guard is a completely offline tower defense game. It is designed to be enjoyed without any network connection, user accounts, or data collection of any kind. Your privacy is fully respected — because we simply do not collect, store, or transmit any information.

Information Collection

Bridge Guard does not collect any personal or non-personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Usage statistics, gameplay analytics, or crash reports
  • Photos, contacts, microphone input, or any on-device data

The app requests no device permissions and makes no network requests.

Data Usage

Since no data is collected, no data is used, shared, sold, or disclosed to any party for any purpose. All gameplay data (such as level progress) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Bridge Guard does not integrate any third-party services. There are:

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks
  • No in-app purchases or payment processors
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud services or external servers

Children's Privacy

Bridge Guard is safe for users of all ages. Because the app collects absolutely no data, it fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide. No special parental consent is required.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Given the offline, data-free nature of this app, material changes are not anticipated.
