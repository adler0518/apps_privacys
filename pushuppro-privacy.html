PushUp Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Overview

PushUp Pro is a fully offline push-up counter application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

PushUp Pro does NOT collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No health data shared with third parties
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No network requests are made

Local Data Storage

Your training records (rep counts, duration, timestamps) are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Proximity Sensor

The app uses your device's built-in proximity sensor solely to detect push-up repetitions. No sensor data is recorded, stored, or transmitted beyond the immediate counting function.

Third-Party Services

PushUp Pro does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, there are no special concerns regarding children's use of this app.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
