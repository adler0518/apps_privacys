Privacy Policy

Word Scramble Neon

Effective Date: May 22, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Word Scramble Neon ("the App") is developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Word Scramble Neon does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or account credentials
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to external servers
  • No payment or financial information
  • No contacts, photos, or media

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App uses Apple's AppStorage / UserDefaults to save the following data locally on your device only:

  • Difficulty level preference
  • Haptic feedback and speech settings
  • Game statistics: total solved, total wrong, total skipped, hints used, best streak, total score

This data never leaves your device and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Word Scramble Neon does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social platforms. There are no third-party services that receive any data.

5. Network Access

The App requires no network access. All 500 words and definitions are bundled within the App. No internet connection is needed at any time.

6. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device and no external servers are used, the security of your information is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID) managed by iOS.

7. Data Deletion

All locally stored data (game stats and preferences) is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App from your device. You may also reset statistics from within the App at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Word Scramble Neon does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
