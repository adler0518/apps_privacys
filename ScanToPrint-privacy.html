Privacy Policy — ScanToPrint

Last updated: June 3, 2026

ScanToPrint ("the App") uses your iPhone's LiDAR sensor to capture 3D scans of real-world objects and prepares them as print-ready 3D models. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information. In short: the App collects no personal data and works entirely offline.

1. Information We Collect

ScanToPrint does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

2. Camera & LiDAR Use

The App requests access to your device camera and LiDAR sensor solely to perform on-device 3D scanning. Captured depth data and meshes are processed entirely on your device. No camera images, depth data, or scans are uploaded, shared, or transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Local Data Storage

Your scanned models, meshes, and project records are stored only on your device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the App removes all of this data.

4. Permissions

Aside from camera access used for scanning, ScanToPrint does not request access to your microphone, location, or contacts. It needs no other special permissions to function.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, cloud servers, or external APIs. It functions completely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of exposure. All app data remains on your device under the protection of iOS.

8. Your Rights

You retain full control of your data. You can delete all locally stored information at any time by removing the App from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
