Privacy Policy

Balance Math — Last updated: May 2026

100% Offline · Zero Data Collection

1. Introduction

Balance Math is a fully offline algebra puzzle app. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices — in short, we collect nothing from you.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Balance Math does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Photos, contacts, or any device content
  • Payment or financial information

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Balance Math stores only your puzzle progress locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible by us or any third party
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Network Access

Balance Math makes zero network requests. The app functions entirely offline and does not connect to any server, API, or analytics service.

5. Third-Party Services

Balance Math uses no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

6. Children's Privacy

Balance Math is appropriate for all ages, including children. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no special privacy considerations for younger users.

7. Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall Balance Math from your device. All locally stored progress is permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date above and note the changes in the app's release notes.
