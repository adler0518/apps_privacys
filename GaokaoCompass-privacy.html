Privacy Policy

GaokaoCompass does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and locally on your device.

GaokaoCompass does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. The app has no network connectivity requirements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

5. Children's Privacy

GaokaoCompass is safe for users of all ages. Since no personal data is collected, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.
