Privacy Policy

App: Bubble  |  Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Bubble is a fully offline arcade platformer game. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: almost none) and how your device is used while playing.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Bubble does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Photos, contacts, microphone, or camera data
  • Financial or payment information

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Bubble stores only the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Game progress — current unlocked level and completed stages
  • Rescued companions — count of companions saved across levels
  • App settings — sound on/off preference, tutorial seen flag

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app permanently removes all stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

Bubble does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or social login services. No data is shared with any third party.

5. Network Access

Bubble requires no internet connection and makes no network requests. All gameplay is fully offline.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no associated security risk from using this app beyond standard iOS device security.

7. Data Deletion

To delete all app data, simply uninstall Bubble from your iPhone. All locally stored game progress will be permanently removed.

8. Children's Privacy

Bubble does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no social features.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
