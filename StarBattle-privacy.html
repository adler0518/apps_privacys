Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Star Battle ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Star Battle mobile application ("App").

Key Point: Star Battle is a 100% offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent: Star Battle does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Financial information
  • Health or fitness data
  • User content or communications

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to any server:

  • Game Progress: Your current puzzle state, including placed stars and marks
  • Game Records: Your best completion times and number of completed games per difficulty level
  • Settings: App preferences and configuration

All of this data is stored using iOS local storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and local files). You can delete all app data by deleting the App from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Star Battle does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks.

The App is built entirely with native iOS technologies (Swift, SwiftUI) and does not integrate with:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Facebook SDK or social media trackers
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any other third-party data collection tools

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. All game data remains securely stored on your device, protected by iOS's built-in security mechanisms.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all app data:

  1. Press and hold the Star Battle app icon on your home screen
  2. Select "Remove App"
  3. Confirm "Delete App"

This will permanently remove all game progress, records, and settings from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Star Battle is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 or any other age group. The App is designed to be a safe, offline puzzle experience for everyone.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
