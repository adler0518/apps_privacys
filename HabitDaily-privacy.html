Privacy Policy

App: Habit Daily
Developer: Xiao Du
Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Habit Daily ("the App") is a cyberpunk-themed daily habit tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Habit Daily does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No name, email, or account required
  • No device identifiers collected
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting sent to servers
  • No advertising SDKs or tracking libraries
  • No network requests of any kind

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All habit data (names, check-in history, streaks, targets, timestamps) is stored locally on your device only, using the iOS document directory. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any external server
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App

4. Third-Party Services

Habit Daily does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external services of any kind.

5. Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, photos, location, contacts, microphone, health data, or any other device permissions.

6. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted over the network and all information remains on your device, your habit data is protected by iOS device security (passcode, Face ID / Touch ID).

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all habit data at any time from the Settings screen within the App ("Clear All Data"). Uninstalling the App also permanently removes all local data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will publish the new version at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
