FlagQuiz Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

FlagQuiz ("we", "our", or "the app") is a world flag quiz and learning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

FlagQuiz does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Health or fitness data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

FlagQuiz stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Quiz progress and scores
  • Daily challenge streaks
  • Per-country mastery statistics

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults and never leaves your device. It is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Network Usage

The app loads flag images from flagcdn.com, a public CDN service for country flag images. This is the only network request the app makes. No personal data is sent in these requests.

5. Third-Party Services

FlagQuiz does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is flagcdn.com for flag image delivery.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data breach from our end. All app data remains on your device, protected by iOS security features.

7. Data Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply uninstall the app. You can also reset progress within the app's settings.

8. Children's Privacy

FlagQuiz is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains educational content about world geography and does not include any inappropriate material.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
