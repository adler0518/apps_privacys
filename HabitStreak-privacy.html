🔥 HabitStreak

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

HabitStreak ("we," "our," or "the App") is a habit-tracking application built for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. Our guiding principle is simple: we collect nothing. Everything you do in HabitStreak stays on your device.

100% Offline & Private

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

HabitStreak does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts, calendar, or photos
  • Any behavioral or advertising data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

HabitStreak stores your habits and completion history locally on your iPhone using Apple's UserDefaults API with JSON encoding. This data includes:

  • Habit names, icons, and colors you define
  • Completion dates for each habit
  • Frequency settings (daily, weekdays, weekly)
  • App preferences (language, theme)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

HabitStreak does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or cloud services. The App makes zero network requests.

5. Data Security

Because all data resides exclusively on your device, its security is governed by your iPhone's built-in protections — device passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, and iOS data-protection encryption. We recommend keeping your iPhone's iOS software up to date to benefit from the latest security improvements.

6. Data Deletion

To permanently delete all HabitStreak data, simply uninstall the App from your iPhone. All locally stored habit records and settings are immediately and irreversibly removed.

7. Children's Privacy

HabitStreak does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13. The App is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version here and update the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
