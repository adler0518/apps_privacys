Privacy Policy

Helix Reflex · Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Helix Reflex (the "App") is a single-player tap-reflex arcade game developed by Xiadu. We respect your privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the App does and does not handle.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No name, email, phone number, or account information
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA) or analytics identifiers
  • No usage tracking, crash reports, or telemetry

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores only your best score locally on the device using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism. This value never leaves your phone and is not associated with any identifier.

4. Third-Party Services

The App contains no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or backend servers. The App does not perform any network requests during normal use.

5. Data Security

Because no data is transmitted off your device, there is no server-side data to protect. Your local best score is sandboxed within the App's container, governed by iOS system-level security.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data created by the App, simply uninstall the App from your device. This removes the local best score and any related local files.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this Privacy Policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and publish the new version at the same URL.
