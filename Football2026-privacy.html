Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

Football 2026 - Match Tracker ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone) are collected
  • No location data is accessed
  • No photos, contacts, or health data are accessed
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking technologies are used

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User preferences (nickname, favorite teams, timezone setting, dark mode preference)
  • Fantasy game data (virtual coin balance, bet history, leaderboard scores)
  • Notification preferences

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server.

Network Requests

The App makes HTTPS requests to public sports data APIs to retrieve match schedules, live scores, team information, and player statistics. These requests do not include any personal information. The APIs accessed include:

  • TheSportsDB (public sports database)
  • ESPN public sports API
  • Football-Data.org (optional, user-provided API key)

Local Notifications

The App may send local notifications (e.g., match start alerts, goal notifications) if you grant permission. Notification scheduling is handled entirely on-device. No push notification server is involved.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The fantasy betting feature uses only virtual currency with no real monetary value.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above.
