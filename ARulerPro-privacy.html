Privacy Policy

ARuler Pro · Last updated: May 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ARuler Pro ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it on your iOS device. ARuler Pro is an offline AR measuring tool. Your privacy is fundamental to how the App is designed.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ARuler Pro does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials (the App does not require an account)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, MAC address)
  • Precise or coarse location
  • Contacts, calendars, reminders, photos library, or files
  • Camera frames, AR session captures, or LiDAR depth data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to third parties

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device:

Data TypePurposeWhere Stored
Measurement records (length, area, angle values)To allow you to revisit past measurementsOn-device SwiftData store
Project metadata (project name, notes, timestamps)To organize your measurementsOn-device SwiftData store
App preferences (units, theme)To remember your settingsOn-device UserDefaults
Subscription receiptTo validate Pro subscription statusiOS Keychain / StoreKit (Apple)

4. Camera Access

ARuler Pro requests Camera permission so that ARKit can render the live AR view used for measurement. The camera feed and any AR/LiDAR depth information are processed entirely on-device in real time and are never recorded, saved, or transmitted. You may revoke camera access at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > ARuler Pro; without it the AR measuring feature cannot operate.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates only Apple system frameworks (ARKit, SceneKit, SwiftUI, SwiftData, StoreKit). It includes no third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, or crash-reporting SDKs.

In-app purchases (Pro subscription) are processed by Apple StoreKit. Apple's handling of those transactions is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device, the security of that data is protected by iOS itself. The App uses standard iOS sandboxing and does not transmit data over a network.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete individual measurements or projects within the App at any time. To delete all data the App has stored, simply uninstall ARuler Pro from your device. iOS will remove the App's sandbox and all associated local data.

8. Children's Privacy

ARuler Pro is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Because the App collects no personal data at all, it complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through an App update.
