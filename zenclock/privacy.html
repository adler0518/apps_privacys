ZenClock Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

ZenClock ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

ZenClock is designed to work entirely offline. The app does not:

  • Require user registration or login
  • Collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Connect to any server or send any data over the internet

2. Data Stored Locally

ZenClock stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • App Settings: Your preferences for clock style, ambient sounds, breathing patterns, and display options
  • Favorite Quotes: Zen quotes you have bookmarked
  • Meditation Records: Session duration and timestamp of your breathing exercises
  • Display History: Records of which quotes have been shown to avoid repetition

All data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

3. Third-Party Services

ZenClock does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

4. Background Audio

ZenClock uses the background audio capability solely to continue playing ambient sounds (such as rain, ocean, or temple bell) during meditation sessions in the Digital Zen Room. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

5. Children's Privacy

ZenClock does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

禅意钟 隐私政策

生效日期：2026年6月4日

禅意钟（"我们"或"本应用"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们在数据收集和使用方面的做法。

1. 我们收集的信息

我们不收集任何个人信息。

禅意钟设计为完全离线运行。本应用不会：

  • 要求用户注册或登录
  • 收集姓名、电子邮件地址或任何个人标识信息
  • 追踪您的位置
  • 访问您的通讯录、照片或其他个人数据
  • 使用 Cookie 或跟踪技术
  • 连接任何服务器或通过互联网发送任何数据

2. 本地存储的数据

禅意钟仅在您的设备本地存储以下数据：

  • 应用设置：您对时钟风格、环境音效、呼吸模式和显示选项的偏好设置
  • 收藏禅语：您收藏的禅语
  • 冥想记录：呼吸练习的会话时长和时间戳
  • 展示历史：已展示禅语的记录，用于避免重复

所有数据均使用 Apple SwiftData 框架存储在您的设备上，不会传输到外部服务器。如果您删除本应用，所有本地存储的数据将被永久删除。

3. 第三方服务

禅意钟不集成任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪服务。我们不会与第三方共享任何数据。

4. 后台音频

禅意钟使用后台音频功能仅用于在数字禅房冥想期间继续播放环境音效（如雨声、海浪声或寺钟声）。不会录制或传输任何音频。

5. 儿童隐私

禅意钟不会有意收集儿童的任何信息。本应用适合所有年龄段的用户使用。

6. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更将在本页面上反映，并更新生效日期。
