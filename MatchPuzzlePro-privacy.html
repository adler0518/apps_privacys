Privacy Policy

App Name: Match Puzzle Pro

Last Updated: 2026-05-20

1. Introduction

Match Puzzle Pro is a puzzle game app that operates entirely offline. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Important: This app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data remains on your device.
  • ✓ No personal information collected
  • ✓ No location data accessed
  • ✓ No contact information required
  • ✓ No financial information processed
  • ✓ No usage analytics tracked

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game progress and completion status
  • Star ratings for completed levels
  • User preferences and settings

All data is stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. There are no advertisements or tracking mechanisms.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by iOS security mechanisms. We do not have access to your data and cannot retrieve it.

6. Data Deletion

All app data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app from your device or reset all progress within the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app description on the App Store.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store support page.
