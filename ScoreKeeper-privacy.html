ScoreKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · 100% Offline · No Tracking

Short version: ScoreKeeper does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the ScoreKeeper app ("we", "our", "the app") handles information when you use it on your iPhone or iPad. By using ScoreKeeper, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We deliberately built ScoreKeeper to require no information about you. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, or any account information
  • Your location (precise or approximate)
  • Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising IDs)
  • Photos, contacts, calendar, microphone, or camera data
  • Crash reports, usage analytics, or behavioral data
  • IP address or any network metadata

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The only data ScoreKeeper produces is your in-progress match state — team names, scores, fouls, and timer values. This data lives in volatile memory while the app is open. Removing the app erases everything.

4. Third-Party Services

ScoreKeeper integrates no third-party services. There are no:

  • Analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • Crash reporting (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Cloud sync providers
  • Login providers (Apple, Google, Facebook, etc.)

5. Permissions

ScoreKeeper requests no system permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, or notifications.

6. Data Security

Because all data is stored on your device and never transmitted, the security of that data depends on your device's security (passcode, biometrics, system encryption). We recommend keeping your iOS up to date and using a device passcode.

7. Data Deletion

To delete all data created by ScoreKeeper, simply delete the app. There is no remote copy of your data on any server.

8. Children's Privacy

ScoreKeeper is suitable for users of all ages. Because we collect no personal information, the app is safe for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if functionality changes. Updates will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will also be reflected in the app's release notes.

10. Contact Us
