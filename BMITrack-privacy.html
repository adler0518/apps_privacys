BMI Track - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Overview

BMI Track is a body mass index calculator and weight tracking application that runs entirely on your device.

Data Collection

BMI Track does not collect any personal information:

  • No account registration required
  • No personal data collected or transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising networks
  • No data leaves your device

Local Data Storage

BMI Track stores weight records, BMI calculations, and user profile settings locally on your device using SwiftData. This includes height, weight entries, goals, and preferences. This data never leaves your device.

CSV Export

You may choose to export your data as a CSV file. This export is initiated only by you and the file remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

Notifications

BMI Track may send local notifications as weight logging reminders. These are processed entirely on-device and no notification data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

BMI Track does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

BMI Track does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
