Privacy Policy

App Name: Gratitude Jar / 感恩罐
Bundle ID: com.xiadu.gratitudejar
Last Updated: May 2026

🔒 This app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information We Collect

Gratitude Jar does not collect any personal information. All gratitude entries, mood data, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AppStorage / JSON). No data is ever sent to external servers.

Data Storage

All app data (journal entries, mood gems, PIN settings, haptic preferences) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data. Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no network requests made by the app.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is fully offline and collects no data from any user.

Shake & Recall Feature

The shake gesture feature uses only the device's built-in accelerometer to detect motion. No sensor data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

PIN Security

The optional PIN lock is stored locally on your device using AppStorage. It is never transmitted or accessible outside your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
