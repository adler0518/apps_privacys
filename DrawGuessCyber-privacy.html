Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cyber Draw. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our iOS drawing & guessing game. The short version: we collect nothing.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, Apple ID)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage statistics or behavioral data
  • Drawings or canvas content
  • Payment or financial information

3. Data Stored on Your Device (Local Only)

Cyber Draw stores the following data exclusively on your device via Apple's UserDefaults. It never leaves your device:

  • Word history: previously seen words to avoid repetition
  • Trophies: your unlocked achievements
  • Settings: difficulty preference, language choice

Deleting the app permanently removes all data.

4. Network Usage

Cyber Draw is 100% offline. No network requests are made. No data is sent anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

Cyber Draw does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, crash reporters, or ad networks.

6. Permissions

Cyber Draw requests no system permissions beyond standard screen rendering and haptic feedback. No camera, microphone, location, photos, or contacts access is required.

7. Children's Privacy

Cyber Draw is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected from any user, there are no special provisions needed for children under 13 (COPPA compliance by design).

8. Data Deletion

Delete the app from your device to permanently remove all associated data.

9. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
