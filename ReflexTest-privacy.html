Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Introduction

ReflexTest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our iOS application.

Summary: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ReflexTest is designed with privacy-first principles. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information
  • Identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • Financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or any other personal data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

ReflexTest stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game scores and reaction times
  • Game history and statistics
  • Daily challenge progress
  • Personal best records

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults and is:

  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Only accessible on your device
100% Offline: ReflexTest operates completely offline. No internet connection is required, and no network requests are made.

4. Third-Party Services

ReflexTest does NOT use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks.

The app is built using only Apple's native iOS frameworks (SwiftUI, Foundation) and contains no external dependencies.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

  • No server-side security measures are needed
  • No data breaches are possible
  • Your game data is as secure as your device

6. Data Deletion

All game data is automatically and permanently deleted when you:

  • Uninstall ReflexTest from your device
  • Delete the app from your iPhone

No data persists after app removal. No account deletion is required because no accounts exist.

7. Children's Privacy

ReflexTest is appropriate for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for children to use without parental concern about data collection or privacy risks.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
