ZenClock - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

Overview

ZenClock ("the App") is developed by Xiadu. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

ZenClock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

  • No account required: The App does not require registration or login.
  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No advertising: The App contains no advertisements or ad-tracking.
  • No network requests: The App does not make network requests for user data.

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using SwiftData:

  • Your favorite zen quotes
  • Meditation session records (duration, date)
  • App preferences (clock style, sound settings)
  • Quote display history

This data is stored solely on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Background Audio

The App uses background audio capability to play ambient sounds during meditation sessions. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

WidgetKit

The App provides home screen widgets that display time and quotes. Widget data is shared between the App and widget extension via App Groups, remaining on-device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no objectionable content and is suitable for all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above.
