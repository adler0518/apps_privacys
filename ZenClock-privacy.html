ZenClock Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

Summary: ZenClock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely offline.

Data Collection

ZenClock does not collect any personal information. All user data, including settings preferences, favorite quotes, and meditation records, is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (UserDefaults and SwiftData).

Network Usage

ZenClock does not use any network connections. The app operates completely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Third-Party Services

ZenClock does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third parties.

Audio Background Mode

ZenClock uses the background audio capability solely to continue playing ambient meditation sounds (such as mountain spring, bamboo wind, temple bell, or rain) when you enter the Zen Room meditation mode. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

App Groups

ZenClock uses an App Group (shared container) to synchronize your clock style and quote preferences between the main app and the StandBy widget. This data remains entirely on your device and is not transmitted externally.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling ZenClock will remove all locally stored data from your device. You can also reset your preferences within the app settings.

Children's Privacy

ZenClock does not collect data from any users, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
