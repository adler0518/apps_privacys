Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

GoalNow ("the App") is a 2026 FIFA World Cup tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

GoalNow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, videos, or media

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store minimal preferences (such as notification settings for match reminders) locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

3. Network Requests

The App fetches publicly available sports data from ESPN's public API endpoints to display match scores, standings, and news. These requests do not include any personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

GoalNow does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking services. The only external data source is ESPN's public sports API.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to secure. All network requests use HTTPS encryption.

6. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device. No data exists on any server to delete.

7. Children's Privacy

GoalNow does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no user-generated content, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
