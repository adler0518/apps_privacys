Privacy Policy for PlotPath

Last updated: June 5, 2026

PlotPath ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a first principle. This policy explains how the App handles your information. In short: PlotPath does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

PlotPath does not collect any personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, and no advertising. Specifically, the App does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, usage data, or any identifiers.

Data Stored on Your Device

All content you create or import — including SVG files, optimized output, layer settings, and app preferences — is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share a file using the standard iOS share sheet.

Files Access

PlotPath lets you import SVG files from the Files app and export optimized SVG files. These operations happen entirely on your device. The App accesses only the files you explicitly select and does not browse, scan, or upload any other files.

Third-Party Services

PlotPath does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are no third parties receiving your data.

Data Security

Because your data stays on your device and is never transmitted, it is protected by your device's own security mechanisms, including device passcode and encryption.

Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data. Deleting a file within the App removes it from your device. Deleting the App removes all associated local data.

Children's Privacy

PlotPath does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
