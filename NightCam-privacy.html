NightCam - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Overview

NightCam is a night photography camera application that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

NightCam does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No data leaves your device

Camera and Photo Library Access

NightCam requires access to:

  • Camera: To capture photos using the device camera hardware. Images are processed locally on your device.
  • Photo Library: To save captured and edited photos to your device photo library. Photos are only saved locally when you choose to export them.

Camera data is processed in real-time on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Local Data Storage

All photos captured with NightCam, along with their EXIF metadata and editing adjustments, are stored locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to any external service.

Third-Party Services

NightCam does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

NightCam does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
