Privacy Policy

Gym Keeper  ·  Effective date: May 15, 2026

100% Offline App

1. Introduction

Gym Keeper ("the App") is developed by AITeam Studio. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices. In short: we collect nothing.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, including:

  • Name, email address, or account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data beyond what you manually enter
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Financial information

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your workout sessions, exercise entries, sets, reps, weights, notes, and settings (weight unit preference) locally using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or cloud services. Zero network requests are made.

5. Health Data

Gym Keeper does not connect to Apple Health or HealthKit. All workout data is stored independently within the App.

6. Data Security

All data resides on your device under iOS sandboxing and encryption. Deleting the App permanently removes all data.

7. Data Deletion

Uninstall Gym Keeper to delete all associated data. No remote deletion request is needed.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any users. It is safe for all age groups.
