CleanQuest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CleanQuest. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CleanQuest ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

CleanQuest is a fully offline application that gamifies household cleaning tasks and knowledge. The App operates entirely on your device with no network connectivity required.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

CleanQuest does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

CleanQuest stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Game session scores and streak records
  • Player profile (level, XP, cleaner title)
  • Room cleaning progress and task completion status
  • Achievement unlock status
  • Daily challenge history

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

4. Third-Party Services

CleanQuest does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud storage services. The App contains zero third-party code.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by the security measures built into your iOS device, including device encryption and app sandboxing. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data interception during transmission.

6. Data Deletion

All app data is stored exclusively on your device. To delete all CleanQuest data, simply delete the App from your device. All associated data will be permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

CleanQuest does not collect any personal information from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
