Parking Finder

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: May 2025

1. Introduction

Parking Finder ("we", "our", or "the App") is a parking spot recording and navigation application for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

Short version: Parking Finder does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, Apple ID)
  • Location history or GPS coordinates (to any server)
  • Photos or camera data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports (to our servers)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, device serial)
  • Financial information

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data created by Parking Finder is stored exclusively on your iPhone using local storage. This includes:

  • Parking Records — GPS coordinates, timestamps, notes, and optional photos you take within the app
  • App Preferences — language settings and display preferences

This data is never uploaded to any server. It exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, which are used only on-device:

5. Third-Party Services

Parking Finder uses Apple MapKit to render maps. MapKit may process tile requests through Apple's servers according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not integrate any other third-party SDK, analytics service, or advertising network.

6. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted to external servers, there is no server-side data storage or risk of a remote data breach. Your parking records are protected by your device's built-in security (iOS encryption, Face ID / Touch ID lock).

7. Data Deletion

You can delete individual parking records at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device permanently.

8. Children's Privacy

Parking Finder is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, it poses no risk to children's privacy.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We will not make changes that retroactively increase data collection without your explicit consent.
